## Problem
Engineering managers at public media organizations struggle to find and hire top engineering talent from underrepresented groups and tech alumni networks, often relying on time-consuming and costly manual sourcing and recruiting methods.

## Target User
Engineering managers at public media organizations with 5-10 open engineering positions and a limited $50-$100 budget per hire for sourcing and recruiting. They need an efficient and cost-effective solution to find and hire qualified candidates from diverse backgrounds.

## Solution
TalentAI is an AI-powered engineering talent sourcing and hiring platform that connects companies with a curated pool of diverse engineering candidates. The platform leverages machine learning algorithms to analyze candidate data from tech alumni networks and underrepresented groups, providing a tailored match for each client's specific needs.

## Revenue Model
Monthly SaaS subscription model, where clients pay a fixed fee based on the number of open positions and the level of service required.

## MVP Format
MVP will feature a basic version of the platform with the following components:

1. Data Ingestion: Import data from tech alumni networks and underrepresented groups.
2. Machine Learning Model: Train an initial model to classify and rank candidates based on relevance.
3. Candidate Database: Store and manage a curated pool of diverse engineering candidates.
4. Client Dashboard: Provide a basic interface for clients to view and manage their candidate pool.
5. Integration with Existing Systems: Allow clients to integrate the platform with their existing applicant tracking systems (ATS).

## Estimated Build Time
12 weeks (approximately 240 development hours)

## Validation Steps
1. Validate the accuracy of the machine learning model by conducting a sample candidate evaluation exercise.
2. Conduct a pilot launch with 5-10 clients to gather feedback and iterate on the product.
3. Track key performance indicators (KPIs) such as time-to-hire, candidate quality, and client satisfaction.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React, TypeScript, and Redux
- Backend: Node.js, Express, and MongoDB
- Machine Learning: TensorFlow.js and Scikit-learn
- Integration: Zapier and API Connect
- Testing: Jest and Cypress
- CI/CD: Jenkins and GitHub Actions
