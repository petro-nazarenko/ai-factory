## Problem
Public media organizations struggle to attract and retain diverse engineering talent, resulting in underrepresentation in senior leadership and a lack of innovative ideas. This problem is exacerbated by lengthy hiring processes and inadequate recruitment strategies.

## Target User
The target user is an Engineering Manager at a public media organization with 5-10 open positions. This user is responsible for sourcing and hiring diverse engineering talent, but lacks the resources and tools to effectively compete with private companies.

## Solution
Diversity-focused Engineering TalentAI is a comprehensive engineering talent database and applicant tracking system that integrates with company career pages and popular job boards. The platform utilizes AI-powered diversity-focused matching algorithms to identify top candidates from underrepresented groups. Our solution provides features such as:

* Diverse talent pipelining and sourcing
* Automated tracking and analysis of candidate diversity metrics
* Customizable job boards and career pages
* Integration with popular job boards and social media platforms

## Revenue Model
The revenue model will be a monthly SaaS subscription, with tiered pricing based on the number of open positions and users. We will also offer a free trial period and discounts for non-profit and public sector organizations.

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product (MVP) will include:

* A diverse talent database with 5000+ profiles
* Basic filtering and search capabilities
* Integration with 2-3 popular job boards and company career pages
* Basic AI-powered matching algorithms

## Estimated Build Time
Estimated build time is 6-8 months, including development, testing, and alpha/beta releases.

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct market research and gather feedback from target users
2. Develop and test the MVP with a small group of beta users
3. Analyze usage metrics and gather feedback for iterative improvements
4. Launch marketing campaigns and outreach programs to the target audience
5. Continuously monitor and evaluate the product's impact on diversity and inclusion in the tech industry

## Tech Stack
- Front-end: React.js
- Back-end: Node.js, Express.js
- Database: MongoDB
- AI/ML: TensorFlow.js
- Integration: Zapier, APIs for job boards and social media platforms
- CI/CD: Jenkins, CircleCI
- Testing: Jest, Enzyme, Cypress
