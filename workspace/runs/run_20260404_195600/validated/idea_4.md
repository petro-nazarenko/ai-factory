## Problem
Freelance recruiters and talent analysts manually search multiple job boards and hiring tools, leading to time-consuming and error-prone candidate research and applicant tracking processes.

## Target User
Freelance recruiters and talent analysts who want to efficiently find, assess, and onboard top talent for their clients or organizations.

## Solution
TalentUnify is an AI-powered talent sourcing platform that integrates with multiple job boards and hiring tools, providing a unified candidate research and applicant tracking experience. The platform utilizes natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning algorithms to suggest relevant candidates, predict candidate viability, and automate applicant tracking processes.

## Revenue Model
TalentUnify operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering tiered pricing plans based on the number of users, job postings, and hiring tools integrated.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include the following features:

* Integration with 5 major job boards and hiring tools
* AI-powered candidate research and suggestion engine
* Basic applicant tracking capabilities
* User interface for recruiters and talent analysts

## Estimated Build Time
8 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct user research and surveys to validate the problem and target user needs.
2. Develop a working prototype of the AI-powered candidate research engine.
3. Integrate TalentUnify with 5 major job boards and hiring tools.
4. Test and refine the user interface and applicant tracking features.

## Tech Stack
- Python for backend development
- Node.js for front-end development
- AWS SageMaker for AI and machine learning
- MongoDB for database storage
