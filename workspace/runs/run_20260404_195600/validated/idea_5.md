## Problem
Remote Team Leads at tech startups or digital agencies struggle to find reliable and skilled Sales Development Representatives (SDRs). The traditional hiring process is time-consuming and resource-intensive, with a high failure rate. Leads must sift through numerous unsuitable candidates, often resulting in delayed hiring processes and lost business opportunities.

## Target User
Remote Team Leads responsible for hiring SDRs at a tech startup or digital agency. This user has experience in managing remote teams but lacks the time or resources to invest in a lengthy hiring process.

## Solution
Streamlines The HiringAI is an AI-powered SaaS platform designed to streamline the hiring process for remote SDRs. The platform curates a pool of pre-screened candidates based on AI-driven algorithms, ensuring that clients receive top talent that matches their specific needs. By reducing the time and effort required for traditional hiring processes, Clients can focus on their core business while maintaining a high-quality team.

## Revenue Model
Monthly SaaS subscription-based pricing. The platform charges clients a monthly fee based on the number of active vacancies, with tiered pricing for high-volume users.

## MVP Format
- **Core features:** AI-powered candidate matching and pre-screening.
- **User interface:** Intuitive platform for Clients to manage vacancies, review candidate profiles, and chat with suitable candidates.
- **Onboarding process:** Easy, self-service onboarding for clients, including a comprehensive walkthrough.

## Estimated Build Time
12 weeks (3 months) for development, testing, and initial deployment.

## Validation Steps
1. **Client testing:** Pilot launch with a small group of clients to gather feedback and refine the platform.
2. **Candidate engagement metrics:** Track engagement metrics (e.g., applicant response rates, interview success rates) to measure platform effectiveness.
3. **Client satisfaction surveys:** Conduct regular surveys to gauge client satisfaction levels and identify areas for improvement.

## Tech Stack
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express.js for server-side logic and APIs.
- **AI/ML:** TensorFlow.js for AI-driven candidate matching and pre-screening.
- **Database:** MongoDB for storing user data and candidate profiles.
- **Frontend:** React.js + Material-UI for a seamless user experience.
