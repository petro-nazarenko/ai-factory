## Problem
Engineering managers at public media organizations struggle to retain top talent due to factors such as lack of purposeful projects, limited opportunities for growth, and insufficient support for employee well-being. This leads to high employee turnover rates, recruitment costs, and difficulties in maintaining a skilled and experienced team.

## Target User
The target user is an engineering manager at a public media organization with 5-10 open positions. They are responsible for hiring, retaining, and developing a team of engineers who can drive the organization's technical goals.

## Solution
Purpose-driven Engineering TalentAI is a SaaS platform that uses AI to match employees with meaningful projects and provides a suite of employee engagement and well-being tools. The platform's key features include:

* AI-powered project matching: Uses natural language processing and machine learning algorithms to match employees with projects that align with their skills, interests, and career goals.
* Employee engagement tools: Offers features such as team-building activities, social recognition, and mentoring programs to foster a positive and inclusive work culture.
* Well-being tools: Provides access to mental health resources, wellness programs, and flexible work arrangements to support employees' physical and mental well-being.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model is based on a monthly SaaS subscription, with tiered pricing based on the number of users and features required.

## MVP Format
The MVP will include the following features:

* AI-powered project matching
* Employee engagement tools (team-building activities, social recognition)
* Basic well-being tools (mental health resources, flexible work arrangements)

## Estimated Build Time
12 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct stakeholder interviews with engineering managers and HR professionals at public media organizations to validate the problem and solution.
2. Develop a prototype of the AI-powered project matching feature and conduct user testing with a small group of engineers.
3. Gather feedback from early adopters and iterate on the design and functionality of the platform.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React, Redux
- Backend: Node.js, Express.js
- Database: PostgreSQL
- AI/ML: TensorFlow.js
- DevOps: Docker, Kubernetes
