## Problem
Many remote startups struggle with creating and maintaining transparent salary structures for their distributed teams. This can lead to issues with employee trust, morale, and retention, as well as difficulties in attracting top talent. Current solutions often involve manual spreadsheets or cumbersome HR software, which can be time-consuming and prone to errors.

## Target User
The target user is the CEO or founder of a remote startup with a growing team. This person is likely responsible for making key decisions about company operations, including compensation and benefits. They value transparency, fairness, and efficiency in their business practices.

## Solution
Transparent Salaries is a software solution that helps companies design, implement, and manage transparent salary structures for their remote teams. The platform provides a user-friendly interface for creating and customizing salary frameworks, as well as tools for communicating salary information to employees and stakeholders. Key features include:

* Salary framework templates and customization options
* Automated salary calculations and scaling
* Real-time reporting and analytics
* Integration with popular HR and payroll software
* Employee self-service portal for salary information and requests

## Revenue Model
The revenue model for Transparent Salaries is a monthly SaaS subscription, with pricing tiers based on the number of employees and features required. Additional revenue streams may include premium support services, customized implementation and training, and partnerships with HR and payroll software providers.

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product (MVP) for Transparent Salaries would include the core features for creating and managing salary frameworks, as well as basic integration with popular HR and payroll software. The MVP would be designed to meet the needs of small to medium-sized remote startups, with plans to expand features and functionality based on user feedback and market demand.

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP version of Transparent Salaries is 12 weeks, assuming a team of 2-3 developers, 1 designer, and 1 project manager.

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct market research and surveys to validate the need for transparent salary software among remote startups.
2. Develop a prototype and gather feedback from potential users and industry experts.
3. Test the MVP with a small group of pilot customers and refine the product based on feedback.
4. Analyze user engagement and retention metrics to inform future development and marketing strategies.

## Tech Stack
- Front-end framework: React
- Back-end framework: Node.js
- Database: PostgreSQL
- Integration tools: Zapier, API connectors for HR and payroll software
- Hosting: Cloud-based (AWS or Google Cloud)
