## Problem
B2B SaaS founders struggle to collect high-quality, video testimonials from their customers, which are essential for building trust and credibility with potential customers. Existing solutions often require customers to install apps or navigate complex recording processes, leading to low response rates and poor video quality.

## Target User
The target user is a B2B SaaS founder with 10-100 customers, looking for an easy and efficient way to collect video testimonials from their satisfied customers. This founder values simplicity, ease of use, and high-quality video recordings.

## Solution
LinkAI is a one-page link that customers can open on their mobile device to record a 60-second testimonial video. No app installation is required, making it easy and convenient for customers to provide their testimonial. The link can be customized with the founder's branding and can be shared via email, SMS, or other communication channels.

## Revenue Model
LinkAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, where founders pay a recurring fee to access the platform and its features. The subscription fee can be tiered based on the number of customers, video recordings, or other relevant metrics.

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product (MVP) for LinkAI includes:
* A customizable one-page link for customers to record their testimonial video
* A simple and intuitive recording interface on the customer's mobile device
* automatic video uploading and storage
* Basic video editing and transcription features
* Integration with popular CRM and marketing automation tools

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP is 40 hours, assuming a team of two developers and one designer.

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with B2B SaaS founders to validate the problem and solution.
2. Create a landing page to gauge interest and collect email addresses from potential customers.
3. Develop a functional prototype and test it with a small group of pilot customers.
4. Analyze customer feedback and iterate on the product to improve its features and user experience.

## Tech Stack
- Front-end framework: React
- Back-end framework: Node.js
- Database: MongoDB
- Video recording and processing: AWS Elemental MediaConvert
- Integration with CRM and marketing automation tools: Zapier and API integrations
