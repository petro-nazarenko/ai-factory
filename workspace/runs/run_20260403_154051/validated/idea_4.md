## Problem
Freelance copywriters often spend a significant amount of time creating proposals for clients, which can be tedious and time-consuming. The process involves manually formatting the brief, adding necessary details, and designing the layout, taking away from the time that could be spent on high-value tasks like writing and strategy.

## Target User
The target user is a freelance copywriter with 3-10 clients, who values efficiency and professionalism in their workflow. They are likely familiar with technology and are looking for a tool to streamline their proposal creation process.

## Solution
ProposalPro is a web app that converts a raw brief into a polished proposal PDF in one click. The app uses AI-powered algorithms to analyze the brief, identify key elements, and generate a well-structured proposal that includes essential sections such as introduction, objectives, scope, timeline, and pricing. The app also includes a library of customizable templates and design options to ensure the proposal looks professional and aligned with the copywriter's brand.

## Revenue Model
ProposalPro operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, where users pay a flat fee to access the app's features and generate unlimited proposals. The subscription model provides a predictable revenue stream and incentivizes the development of new features and improvements to the app.

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product (MVP) for ProposalPro includes the following features:
- Brief analysis and proposal generation
- Customizable template library
- Design options for proposal layout and branding
- PDF export
- User dashboard for managing proposals and clients

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP is 160 hours, broken down into:
- Front-end development: 40 hours
- Back-end development: 60 hours
- AI algorithm development: 30 hours
- Testing and debugging: 30 hours

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct user interviews with freelance copywriters to validate the problem and solution.
2. Create a landing page to gauge interest and collect email addresses from potential users.
3. Develop a functional prototype and test it with a small group of users to gather feedback.
4. Refine the app based on user feedback and iterate on the development process.

## Tech Stack
- Front-end: React, CSS, HTML
- Back-end: Node.js, Express
- AI algorithm: Natural Language Processing (NLP) library such as spaCy or Stanford CoreNLP
- Database: MongoDB
- PDF generation: jsPDF or similar library
