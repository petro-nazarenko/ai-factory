## Problem
Shopify store owners earning $10k-$100k/month struggle to keep track of their competitors' prices, making it challenging to stay competitive in the market. Manual monitoring of competitor prices is time-consuming and prone to errors.

## Target User
The target user is a Shopify store owner earning between $10,000 and $100,000 per month. They are likely to be interested in staying competitive, optimizing their pricing strategy, and increasing their revenue.

## Solution
Competitor Price Watch is a lightweight SaaS that monitors up to 20 competitor URLs and sends a daily price delta report via email. The report highlights price changes, allowing store owners to adjust their pricing strategy and stay competitive.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model is based on a monthly SaaS subscription. Users can choose from different plans, with the basic plan including monitoring of up to 5 competitor URLs and the premium plan including monitoring of up to 20 competitor URLs.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) format includes:
- User dashboard to input competitor URLs
- Daily email report with price delta changes
- Basic plan with monitoring of up to 5 competitor URLs

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP is 40 hours, broken down into:
- Frontend development: 10 hours
- Backend development: 15 hours
- Testing and debugging: 10 hours
- Deployment: 5 hours

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with Shopify store owners to validate the problem and solution.
2. Create a landing page to gauge interest and collect email addresses from potential users.
3. Develop a prototype and test it with a small group of users to gather feedback.
4. Analyze user feedback and iterate on the product to improve its functionality and user experience.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React.js
- Backend: Node.js with Express.js
- Database: MongoDB
- Email service: Sendgrid
- Web scraping library: Puppeteer
