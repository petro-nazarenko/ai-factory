## Problem
B2B SaaS founders struggle to collect high-quality, authentic customer testimonials, which are crucial for building trust and credibility with potential customers. Existing solutions often require customers to install apps, navigate complex websites, or dedicate significant time to recording testimonials.

## Target User
B2B SaaS founders with 10-100 customers, who need an easy and efficient way to collect customer testimonials to showcase on their website, social media, or sales materials.

## Solution
LinkAI Testimonial Collector is a one-page link that customers can open on their mobile devices to record a 60-second testimonial video without needing to install any apps. The link can be easily shared via email, SMS, or messaging platforms, making it convenient for customers to provide feedback.

## Revenue Model
LinkAI Testimonial Collector operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, where founders pay a recurring fee to access the platform and collect testimonials from their customers. The subscription fee can be tiered based on the number of customers, features, or storage space required.

## MVP Format
The simplest shippable version of LinkAI Testimonial Collector will include:
- A mobile-optimized one-page link for customers to record testimonials
- A basic video recording and uploading functionality
- A dashboard for founders to view, manage, and download collected testimonials
- Integration with popular SaaS tools for seamless sharing and embedding of testimonials

## Estimated Build Time
40 hours

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with B2B SaaS founders to validate the need for an easy testimonial collection solution.
2. Develop a minimum viable product (MVP) and test it with a small group of pilot customers.
3. Gather feedback and iterate on the product to improve user experience and functionality.
4. Develop a marketing strategy to reach the target audience and acquire paying customers.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React or Angular for building the mobile-optimized one-page link
- Backend: Node.js or Django for handling video uploads, processing, and storage
- Database: PostgreSQL or MongoDB for storing customer information and testimonials
- API Integrations: Zapier or Integromat for integrating with popular SaaS tools
- Storage: AWS S3 or Google Cloud Storage for storing video testimonials
