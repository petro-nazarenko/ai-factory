## Problem
B2B SaaS founders struggle to collect high-quality, authentic customer testimonials, which are crucial for building trust and converting leads. Existing solutions often require customers to install apps, navigate complex recording processes, or dedicate more time than they are willing to give.

## Target User
The target user is a B2B SaaS founder with a customer base ranging from 10 to 100 clients. These founders understand the importance of social proof in their marketing strategies but face challenges in collecting and showcasing customer testimonials.

## Solution
LinkAI is a one-page link that customers can open on their mobile devices to record a 60-second testimonial video without needing to install any app. This streamlined process increases the likelihood of customers participating, as it is quick, easy, and doesn't require any additional downloads.

## Revenue Model
LinkAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, where B2B SaaS founders pay a recurring fee to use the platform. The subscription could offer different tiers with varying features, such as the number of testimonial requests that can be sent per month, advanced analytics, or customizable branding options.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) of LinkAI would include:
- A simple, one-page mobile-optimized website for customers to record their testimonial videos.
- A user dashboard for B2B SaaS founders to create and manage testimonial requests, view recorded videos, and download them for use on their websites or social media channels.
- Basic analytics to track the number of requests sent, videos recorded, and a simple rating system for the quality of the testimonials.

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP version of LinkAI could be around 40-60 hours, depending on the complexity of the features and the technology stack chosen. This includes front-end development for the customer-facing page and the founder's dashboard, back-end development for handling video uploads and storage, and initial testing.

## Validation Steps
1. **Customer Discovery**: Interview B2B SaaS founders to validate the need for an easy, app-less testimonial video recording solution.
2. **Competitor Analysis**: Research existing solutions to understand their shortcomings and how LinkAI can differentiate itself.
3. **Prototype Testing**: Create a basic prototype and test it with a small group of potential users to gather feedback on usability and the value proposition.
4. **Pilot Launch**: Launch a pilot version with a limited number of early adopters to refine the product based on real-world usage data and feedback.

## Tech Stack
- **Front-end**: React or Vue.js for building the user interface, ensuring it is mobile-friendly and responsive.
- **Back-end**: Node.js with Express for handling server-side logic, including video processing and storage.
- **Database**: MongoDB for storing testimonial videos and associated metadata.
- **Storage**: AWS S3 for hosting the video files, ensuring scalability and reliability.
- **Authentication**: OAuth or similar for secure authentication of B2B SaaS founders accessing their dashboards.
