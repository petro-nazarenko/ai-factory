## Problem
B2B SaaS founders struggle to collect high-quality, authentic testimonials from their customers, which are crucial for building trust and driving sales. Existing solutions often require customers to install an app or go through a lengthy process, leading to low response rates.

## Target User
The target user is a B2B SaaS founder with 10-100 customers, looking for an easy and efficient way to collect testimonial videos from their satisfied customers.

## Solution
One-page LinkAI is a simple, mobile-friendly solution that allows customers to record a 60-second testimonial video with just one click, without requiring any app installation. The link can be shared via email, SMS, or any other messaging channel, making it easy for customers to provide their testimonials on-the-go.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model is based on a monthly SaaS subscription, where B2B SaaS founders pay a recurring fee to use the One-page LinkAI platform and collect testimonial videos from their customers.

## MVP Format
The simplest shippable version of One-page LinkAI includes:
- A mobile-friendly, one-page link that customers can open to record a 60-second video
- A basic video recording interface with a countdown timer and a "submit" button
- A dashboard for B2B SaaS founders to view and manage collected testimonials

## Estimated Build Time
40 hours

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with B2B SaaS founders to validate the need for a simple testimonial collection solution.
2. Test the one-page link with a small group of customers to ensure a smooth user experience.
3. Analyze the response rate and quality of collected testimonials to refine the solution.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React or Angular for building the mobile-friendly interface
- Backend: Node.js or Ruby on Rails for handling video uploads and storage
- Storage: AWS S3 or Google Cloud Storage for storing collected videos
- Authentication: OAuth or JWT for securing the dashboard and ensuring only authorized access
