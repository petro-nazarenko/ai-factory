## Problem
B2B SaaS founders struggle to collect high-quality, video testimonials from their customers, which are essential for building trust and credibility with potential customers. The process of collecting testimonials is often manual, time-consuming, and yields low response rates.

## Target User
The target user is a B2B SaaS founder with 10-100 customers, who wants to showcase customer satisfaction and build social proof to attract new customers.

## Solution
LinkAI is a one-page link that customers can open on their mobile devices to record a 60-second testimonial video. No app installation is required, making it easy and convenient for customers to provide feedback. The link can be customized with the SaaS founder's branding and can be shared via email, SMS, or other communication channels.

## Revenue Model
LinkAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, where B2B SaaS founders pay a recurring fee to use the platform and collect testimonial videos from their customers.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include the following features:
- A customizable one-page link for recording testimonial videos
- Mobile optimization for easy video recording
- Basic video editing and trimming capabilities
- Video storage and hosting
- Integration with popular email marketing tools for easy sharing

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP is 40 hours, assuming a team of two developers and one designer.

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with B2B SaaS founders to validate the need for a convenient testimonial collection tool.
2. Test the one-page link with a small group of customers to ensure usability and gather feedback.
3. Analyze competitors and similar solutions to identify areas for differentiation.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React or Angular for building the customizable one-page link
- Backend: Node.js or Ruby on Rails for handling video uploads and storage
- Database: MongoDB or PostgreSQL for storing customer information and video metadata
- Video editing and hosting: AWS Elemental or Vimeo API for handling video processing and hosting
- Integration: Zapier or MuleSoft for integrating with email marketing tools and other third-party services
