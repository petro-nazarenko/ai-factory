## Problem
Freelance copywriters often spend a significant amount of time converting raw briefs into polished proposals, which can be tedious and time-consuming. This process involves formatting, reorganizing, and refining the content to create a professional-looking document. As a result, copywriters may have to dedicate a substantial portion of their work hours to administrative tasks, taking away from their core writing work and potentially affecting their productivity and earning potential.

## Target User
The target user for ProposalPro is a freelance copywriter with 3-10 clients, who values efficiency and professionalism in their work. These copywriters are likely to be sole proprietors or small business owners, working with a variety of clients across different industries. They are proficient in writing and have a good understanding of the brief-to-proposal process but struggle with the time-consuming and tedious aspects of creating polished proposals.

## Solution
ProposalPro is a web-based application that streamlines the proposal creation process for freelance copywriters. The app takes a raw brief (pasted text) as input and converts it into a polished proposal PDF in one click. The app uses AI-powered algorithms to analyze the brief, identify key elements, and format the content into a professional-looking document. The output PDF includes essential elements such as a cover page, introduction, scope of work, timeline, and pricing.

## Revenue Model
ProposalPro operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering users access to the web app and its features for a fixed monthly fee. The subscription includes a limited number of proposal conversions per month, with the option to upgrade to a higher tier for additional conversions. This revenue model provides a predictable and recurring revenue stream for the business.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) format for ProposalPro includes the following features:
- Raw brief input field
- One-click proposal conversion button
- Customizable proposal template
- Basic AI-powered formatting and analysis
- PDF output

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for ProposalPro is 160 hours, broken down into:
- Front-end development: 40 hours
- Back-end development: 60 hours
- AI algorithm development: 30 hours
- Testing and debugging: 30 hours
