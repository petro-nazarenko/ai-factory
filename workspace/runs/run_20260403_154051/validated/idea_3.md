## Problem
B2B SaaS founders struggle to collect high-quality, engaging customer testimonials, which are essential for building credibility and attracting new customers. Existing solutions often require customers to install apps, navigate complex recording processes, or dedicate significant time, leading to low response rates and poor-quality submissions.

## Target User
The target user is a B2B SaaS founder with 10–100 customers, seeking an efficient and user-friendly way to collect authentic, video-based testimonials from their satisfied clients. This founder values simplicity, ease of use, and the ability to showcase customer success stories in a compelling, video format.

## Solution
LinkAI is a one-page link solution that allows customers to record a 60-second testimonial video directly on their mobile devices, without requiring any app installation. This streamlined process ensures a high response rate and facilitates the collection of genuine, high-quality video testimonials. The platform provides an intuitive interface for customers to record, review, and submit their videos, making it easy for SaaS founders to build a library of engaging, video-based testimonials.

## Revenue Model
LinkAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering tiered pricing based on the number of customers, video recordings, or features required. This recurring revenue stream provides a predictable and scalable income source for the business. Additional revenue opportunities may include premium features, such as advanced video editing, testimonial analytics, or customized branding options.

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product (MVP) for LinkAI includes:
1. A simple, one-page link for customers to access and record their testimonial videos.
2. Basic video recording and submission functionality.
3. A dashboard for SaaS founders to view, manage, and download submitted testimonials.
4. Integration with popular SaaS platforms for seamless customer data synchronization.

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP is 40 hours, assuming a small team of experienced developers and designers. This timeframe includes the development of the core features, testing, and initial deployment.

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with B2B SaaS founders to validate the need for a simple, app-free testimonial recording solution.
2. Create a landing page to gauge interest and collect email addresses from potential customers.
3. Develop a functional prototype to test the recording and submission process with a small group of pilot users.
4. Analyze feedback and iterate on the solution to ensure it meets the needs of the target user base.

## Tech Stack
- Front-end framework: React or Vue.js
- Back-end framework: Node.js with Express.js
- Database: MongoDB or PostgreSQL
- Video recording and processing: Utilize WebRTC and cloud-based video encoding services (e.g., AWS Elementary MediaConvert)
- Authentication and authorization: Implement OAuth or JWT-based authentication for secure access control
