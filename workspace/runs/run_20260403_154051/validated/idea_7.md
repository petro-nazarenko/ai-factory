## Problem
Freelance copywriters often spend a significant amount of time creating proposals for their clients, which can be tedious and time-consuming. The process involves reading through the brief, understanding the requirements, and then crafting a polished proposal that showcases their services and pricing. This can take away from the time they have available to focus on high-value tasks such as writing and strategy.

## Target User
The target user for ProposalPro is a freelance copywriter who has 3-10 clients and is looking to streamline their workflow. They are likely to be self-motivated, tech-savvy, and interested in finding ways to reduce administrative tasks and focus on high-leverage activities.

## Solution
ProposalPro is a web app that uses AI to convert a raw brief into a polished proposal PDF in one click. The user simply pastes the brief into the app, and the AI engine analyzes the text and generates a professional-looking proposal that includes all the necessary elements, such as introduction, scope of work, timeline, and pricing.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model for ProposalPro is a monthly SaaS subscription. Users can sign up for a free trial to test the app, and then subscribe to a monthly plan to continue using the service. The subscription fee will be competitive with other SaaS solutions for freelancers and will offer different tiers of service to accommodate different user needs.

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product (MVP) for ProposalPro will include the following features:
- A simple and intuitive user interface for uploading briefs and generating proposals
- An AI engine that can analyze briefs and generate proposals based on a set of pre-defined templates
- A library of pre-built templates for different types of proposals (e.g. blog posts, social media campaigns, etc.)
- Basic customization options for users to personalize their proposals

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP version of ProposalPro is 40 hours for the front-end and 60 hours for the back-end, for a total of 100 hours.

## Validation Steps
To validate the concept for ProposalPro, we will take the following steps:
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with freelance copywriters to understand their pain points and workflow challenges.
2. Develop a landing page to gauge interest in the product and collect email addresses from potential users.
3. Create a minimum viable product (MVP) and test it with a small group of beta users to gather feedback and iterate on the design.

## Tech Stack
- Front-end: React, CSS, JavaScript
- Back-end: Node.js, Express, MongoDB
- AI Engine: Natural Language Processing (NLP) library such as spaCy or NLTK
- Template Engine: Template rendering library such as Handlebars or Mustache
