## Problem
Freelance copywriters often spend a significant amount of time creating customized proposals for their clients. This process involves manually editing and polishing raw briefs, which can be tedious and time-consuming. As a result, copywriters may struggle to efficiently manage their workload, leading to lost productivity and potential revenue.

## Target User
The target user for ProposalPal is a freelance copywriter with 3-10 clients. This user values their time and is looking for ways to streamline their workflow, allowing them to focus on high-value tasks such as writing and strategy. They are likely familiar with technology and are willing to adopt a new tool if it can help them save time and improve their overall productivity.

## Solution
ProposalPal is a web-based application that uses AI-powered technology to convert raw briefs into polished proposal PDFs in one click. The user simply pastes the brief into the app, selects a few customization options, and clicks a button to generate a professional-looking proposal. The app can learn the user's preferences over time, allowing it to produce proposals that are tailored to their specific needs and branding.

## Revenue Model
ProposalPal operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model. Users can choose from different tiers, each with its own set of features and limitations. The basic tier may include a limited number of proposal generations per month, while the premium tier offers unlimited generations, additional customization options, and priority support.

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product (MVP) for ProposalPal includes the following features:
- Raw brief input field
- Basic customization options (e.g., font, color scheme)
- One-click proposal generation
- Downloadable PDF output
- User authentication and subscription management

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP is 40 hours, broken down into:
- Front-end development: 15 hours
- Back-end development: 10 hours
- AI model integration: 5 hours
- Testing and debugging: 10 hours

## Validation Steps
To validate the idea, we will:
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with freelance copywriters to understand their pain points and workflow.
2. Create a landing page and collect email addresses from interested users.
3. Develop a basic prototype and test it with a small group of users.
4. Analyze user feedback and iterate on the product before launching the full MVP.

## Tech Stack
- Front-end: React, Material-UI
- Back-end: Node.js, Express
- AI Model: TensorFlow, Natural Language Processing libraries
- Database: MongoDB
- Storage: AWS S3
- Authentication: OAuth, Passport.js
