## Problem
Shopify store owners, earning between $10k-$100k/month, struggle to stay competitive in their market by tracking price changes of their competitors. This can lead to missed sales and lost revenue opportunities.

## Target User
Target users are Shopify store owners who sell products online and aim to stay competitive in their market by tracking price changes of their competitors. They are tech-savvy, but may not have extensive experience with automation or data analysis.

## Solution
Competitor Price Tracker is a lightweight SaaS that monitors up to 20 competitor URLs and emails a daily price delta report. The tool uses web scraping techniques to extract price data from competitor websites and provides a simple, intuitive interface for users to manage their tracked competitors and view daily price reports.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model is a monthly SaaS subscription, with tiered pricing based on the number of competitor URLs tracked (Basic: 5 URLs, $29/mo; Pro: 20 URLs, $99/mo).

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include the following features:

- Web scraper to extract price data from up to 20 competitor URLs
- Simple interface for users to manage their tracked competitors
- Daily email reports with price delta information
- Basic analytics for users to view price trends

## Estimated Build Time
8-12 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct market research to validate the target user and problem (e.g., surveys, interviews)
2. Develop a prototype and test with a small group of target users
3. Gather feedback and iterate on the MVP based on user input

## Tech Stack
- Programming languages: Python, JavaScript
- Web scraping libraries: Beautiful Soup, Scrapy
- Email service: Mailgun or Sendgrid
- Database: MongoDB or PostgreSQL
- Frontend framework: Bootstrap or Tailwind CSS
- Backend framework: Flask or Django
