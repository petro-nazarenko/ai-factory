## Problem
Many B2B SaaS founders struggle to collect authentic customer testimonials to boost sales and credibility. Existing solutions require customers to download and install apps, create accounts, or invest too much time.

## Target User
B2B SaaS founders with 10-100 customers who regularly face the challenges of collecting customer testimonials.

## Solution
LinkAI is a one-page SaaS solution that enables customers to record a 60-second video testimonial directly from their mobile browser, without the need for any app installations. Founders create a unique link, send it to their customers, and review the recorded videos in a centralized dashboard.

## Revenue Model
LinkAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, with tiered pricing based on the number of customers, video storage, and additional features. The pricing will range from $50/month for small businesses to $500/month for large enterprises.

## MVP Format
The MVP will include:

* A simple, one-page link where customers can record their videos
* A basic video editor for trimming and cropping
* A centralized dashboard for founders to view and manage recorded videos
* Integration with popular email marketing tools for seamless invitation

## Estimated Build Time
10-12 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct user research and interviews with 10+ B2B SaaS founders to understand their pain points and requirements.
2. Create low-fidelity wireframes and prototype to test with a smaller group of 20+ founders.
3. Conduct A/B testing with a minimum of 50 founders to refine the MVP.

## Tech Stack
- Front-end: React.js, WebRTC for video recording
- Back-end: Node.js, Express.js for server-side logic and storage
- Database: MongoDB for storing video metadata and customer data
- Integration: Zapier or Webhooks for seamless integration with popular email marketing tools
