## Problem
Freelance copywriters struggle to craft compelling proposals that showcase their skills and win new clients. Manually drafting PDF proposals is time-consuming, requiring multiple iterations and formatting. This process hinders their ability to compete in a crowded market and maintain a high volume of potential clients.

## Target User
The target user is a freelance copywriter with 3-10 clients, working extensively in content marketing, branding, or communications. They seek a streamlined process for creating professional-grade proposals to differentiate themselves and secure new projects.

## Solution
PitchGenie is a web app AI that automates the proposal generation process. Users simply paste a raw brief into the platform, and the AI engine creates a well-structured and polished PDF proposal in one click. The proposal is customizable with user-selected templates, design elements, and industry-relevant content.

## Revenue Model
PitchGenie operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model. Users can choose from various plans, with increased storage, template choices, and priority support available in higher-tier subscriptions.

## MVP Format
Minimum Viable Product (MVP):

* AI-powered proposal generation from raw briefs
* Limited template library with minimal design options
* Integrations with Google Drive and Dropbox for brief upload
* Optional: Basic proposal analytics for user performance tracking

## Estimated Build Time
8 weeks (with 2 weeks of planning and 4 weeks of AI model development and 2 weeks for MVP build)

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct market research and iterate on the MVP based on target user feedback.
2. Develop the AI model with a large dataset of standard proposal formats and common brief types.
3. Integrate with major cloud storage providers.
4. Conduct usability and performance testing.
5. Launch public preview and gather real-user feedback.

## Tech Stack
- Web app: Next.js and Vercel
- AI: TensorFlow.js for proposal generation
- Database: PostgreSQL for large-scale brief storage
- Storage Integrations: Google Drive and Dropbox
- Templating Engine: Handlebars
- Analytics: Google Analytics
