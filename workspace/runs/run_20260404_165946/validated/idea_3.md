## Problem
B2B SaaS founders struggle to collect high-quality customer testimonials, leading to difficulty in marketing and sales efforts. Current solutions require time-consuming and invasive onboarding processes, resulting in low adoption rates.

## Target User
B2B SaaS founders with 10-100 customers who want to leverage customer testimonials for marketing and sales purposes.

## Solution
LinkAI is a one-page link that customers open on their mobile devices to record a 60-second testimonial video. This solution eliminates the need for app installs or complex onboarding processes, making it easy for customers to provide high-quality testimonials.

## Revenue Model
LinkAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, with pricing tiers based on the number of customers.

## MVP Format
MVP features:

* One-page link for customers to access the testimonial recording feature
* Automated video hosting and sharing
* Basic analytics and reporting
* Integration with popular customer management platforms

## Estimated Build Time
6 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct user interviews with B2B SaaS founders to validate the problem and gather requirements.
2. Create a prototype and test with 10-20 users to gather feedback and iterate on the design.
3. Develop the MVP and conduct a pilot with 5-10 B2B SaaS companies to validate the solution.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React, TypeScript
- Backend: Node.js, Express
- Database: PostgreSQL
- Video recording and hosting: Third-party API (e.g. Vimeo, YouTube)
- Analytics and reporting: Google Tag Manager, Google Analytics
- Integration with customer management platforms: Zapier, APIs
