## Problem
B2B SaaS founders struggle to collect high-quality customer testimonials to showcase on their websites and marketing materials. Current methods include sending customers to a separate website, emailing links, or even physically collecting video evidence. These methods are often time-consuming, low-quality, and ineffective.

## Target User
B2B SaaS founders with 10-100 customers, managing their marketing efforts to acquire new customers and drive revenue growth.

## Solution
One-Page LinkAI is a simple one-page link customers open on their mobile devices to record a 60-second testimonial video, no app installation required. It uses AI-powered video editing to trim and optimize the video for maximum engagement. The founder can customize the testimonial request, branding, and link to fit their marketing strategy.

## Revenue Model
One-Page LinkAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering different tiers with varying features and support, such as:

- Basic: Limited number of testimonials, basic customization
- Pro: Unlimited testimonials, advanced customization, priority support
- Enterprise: Custom branding, advanced analytics, dedicated support

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product (MVP) will include:

- A single-page link for customers to access
- AI-powered video recording and editing
- Basic customization options (text, background, colors)
- Limited number of testimonials
- Integration with existing marketing automation tools

## Estimated Build Time
6 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct user surveys among B2B SaaS founders to gauge interest and pain points.
2. Conduct A/B testing of different video templates and customization options.
3. Analyze the efficiency and quality of testimonials collected using One-Page LinkAI.
4. Gather feedback from early adopters to refine the product and improve customer satisfaction.

## Tech Stack
- Front-end: React, webAssembly for video editing
- Back-end: Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB
- AI-powered video editing: Custom integration with existing video processing APIs
- Marketing automation integration: Zapier, Google Tag Manager
- Design System: Tailwind CSS
