## Problem

B2B SaaS founders struggle to collect high-quality customer testimonials for marketing and fundraising purposes. Current solutions often require lengthy setup, installations, or even dedicated hardware. These complications discourage potential users and limit the effectiveness of testimonials.

## Target User

B2B SaaS founders with 10-100 customers. This target user segment faces difficulties in collecting testimonials from a moderate-sized customer base.

## Solution

One-Page LinkAI is a mobile-optimized, web-based solution that enables the target user to easily collect 60-second video testimonials from their customers. The service allows users to create custom one-page links and share them with their customers via email, SMS, or messaging apps.

Upon visiting the one-page link, customers will be instructed on how to share their testimonial. The product will seamlessly record, edit, and render high-quality video testimonials that can be easily embedded into marketing materials.

## Revenue Model

One-Page LinkAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model. Pricing will be tiered based on the number of customers and the number of testimonials collected per month.

## MVP Format

The minimum viable product (MVP) for One-Page LinkAI will consist of:

1. A mobile-friendly front-end framework for creating and sharing one-page links.
2. Back-end APIs for video recording and editing utilizing the Web API.
3. Integration with cloud-based storage services for video rendering.
4. A simple analytics dashboard for users to track their customer responses.

## Estimated Build Time

5 - 7 days for the MVP format.

## Validation Steps

1. User testing: recruit 10 B2B SaaS founders and evaluate the usability and effectiveness of One-Page LinkAI in collecting customer testimonials.
2. Sales outreach: target 5 B2B SaaS companies and gauge interest in One-Page LinkAI to estimate the demand for the product.

## Tech Stack

- Front-end: React, Next.js, Tailwind CSS
- Back-end: Node.js, Express.js, API endpoints utilizing Web API for video recording and editing
- Database: Firebase Realtime Database or a cloud-based NoSQL database
- Storage: AWS S3 or Google Cloud Storage for video rendering
- Analytics: Google Analytics or Mixpanel
- Deployment: AWS or Google Cloud Platform
