## Problem
Shopify store owners face difficulty in tracking price changes on competitor URLs, leading to missed opportunities and revenue loss. The current solutions often require manual effort and do not scale.

## Target User
Shopify store owners who earn $10,000 to $100,000 per month, targeting the e-commerce space. This demographic has the highest potential to benefit from competitive pricing analysis.

## Solution
PriceMonitor Pro is a lightweight SaaS that enables Shopify store owners to monitor up to 20 competitor URLs and receive a daily price delta report. It provides valuable insights on competitor pricing, helping store owners stay competitive in the market.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model is based on a monthly SaaS subscription. The pricing tier will be divided into three levels: Basic ($29/month, max 3 competitor URLs), Pro ($99/month, max 10 competitor URLs), and Enterprise ($299/month, max 20 competitor URLs).

## MVP Format
The MVP version of PriceMonitor Pro will include the following features:

1. Monitor up to 10 competitor URLs
2. Daily price delta report via email
3. Real-time competitor URL crawling

## Estimated Build Time
15 days, with the following resource allocations:

* Research and planning: 2 days
* Development: 6 days
* Testing and debugging: 3 days
* Deployment and marketing: 4 days

## Validation Steps
To validate the MVP, we will execute the following steps:

1. Conduct surveys with 50 Shopify store owners to gauge interest and validate the pricing tier.
2. Host a pilot program with 20 store owners to test the MVP version.
3. Analyze the results from the pilot program and make adjustments to the solution as necessary.

## Tech Stack
- Web framework: Flask or Django
- Database: SQLite or PostgreSQL
- Scraping library: Scrapy or BeautifulSoup
- Email service: SendGrid
- UI library: React or Angular
