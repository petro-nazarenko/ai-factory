## Problem
B2B SaaS founders struggle to collect authentic customer testimonials, which hinder their marketing efforts. Existing solutions require customers to download and install a mobile app, which can be a barrier to adoption.

## Target User
B2B SaaS founders with 10-100 customers who need authentic testimonials to enhance their marketing and sales strategies.

## Solution
LinkAI is a one-page link that customers open on their mobile device to record a 60-second video testimonial without any app installation. This solution simplifies the process for customers while providing B2B SaaS founders with a seamless way to collect authentic testimonials.

## Revenue Model
LinkAI follows a monthly SaaS subscription model. Customers are charged based on the number of testimonial videos collected or a flat fee per campaign.

## MVP Format
MVP will include:
- One-page link for customers to record video testimonials
- Integration with popular video hosting platforms for seamless sharing
- Basic analytics for tracking testimonial views and engagement
- Simple dashboard for founders to manage and share testimonials

## Estimated Build Time
12-16 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct market research with 5-10 B2B SaaS founders to validate the problem and solution.
2. Test the MVP with a small pilot group of 10-20 customers to ensure seamless user experience.
3. Gather feedback and iterate on the solution to improve its effectiveness.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React or Angular for building the one-page link
- Backend: Node.js with Express.js for handling user authentication, video hosting integration, and analytics
- No-code tools for setting up and managing integrations
- Cloud hosting for scalable and secure deployment
