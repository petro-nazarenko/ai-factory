## Problem
Freelance copywriters spend too much time crafting personalized proposals for each client, taking away from the time they can spend on writing actual copy. This time-consuming process can lead to missed deadlines, lost clients, and decreased revenue.

## Target User
Freelance copywriters with 3–10 clients who struggle with proposal writing, are looking to streamline their workflow and free up time to focus on growing their business.

## Solution
ProposeAI is a web app that converts a raw brief (pasted text) into a polished proposal PDF in one click. Our AI-powered solution uses natural language processing and machine learning algorithms to analyze the brief, create a tailored proposal template, and automatically populate relevant details.

## Revenue Model
ProposeAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering users three pricing tiers:

- Basic ($19/month): Limited to 5 proposals per month
- Premium ($49/month): Unlimited proposals, access to additional template library
- Pro ($99/month): Unlimited proposals, access to VIP support, priority template updates

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product will include:

- A user-friendly interface for uploading a raw brief
- Automatic proposal generation in standard formats (Word, PDF)
- Basic AI-powered template selection (industry-specific templates)
- Optional: integrations with project management tools and CRM platforms

## Estimated Build Time
Estimated build time: 4 weeks, consisting of 2 weeks for the AI development and 2 weeks for the web app frontend integration.

## Validation Steps
1. User testing sessions to gather feedback on the proposal generation and template selection features.
2. A/B testing of different pricing tiers and subscription models to determine the most effective revenue strategy.
3. Analyze key performance indicators (KPIs) such as proposal generation time, user satisfaction and retention rates to continuously improve the product.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React
- Backend: Node.js (Express)
- Database: MongoDB
- AI Framework: TensorFlow.js
- Cloud infrastructure: AWS lambda, AWS S3
