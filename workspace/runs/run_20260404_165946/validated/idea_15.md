## Problem
B2B SaaS founders struggle to gather authentic customer testimonials, slowing down their marketing efforts and limiting their growth opportunities. Existing methods, such as in-person interviews or lengthy surveys, are time-consuming, may result in low response rates, or provide poor video quality.

## Target User
The target user is a B2B SaaS founder with 10-100 customers, actively acquiring new customers, and seeking to scale their business through social proof and word-of-mouth marketing.

## Solution
LinkAI is a one-page link that end-users open on their mobile device to record a 60-second video testimonial. Upon opening the link, users are guided through a seamless and short registration process, then presented with a simple and user-friendly video recording interface. They can record themselves sharing their experience and benefits of using the SaaS product, including any issues they faced and how the product helped resolve them.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model for LinkAI will be a monthly SaaS subscription with tiered pricing plans (e.g., basic, premium, enterprise), dependent on the number of users, features, or analytics required.

## MVP Format
Minimum Viable Product (MVP) of LinkAI would consist of:

* Front-end UI/UX for a seamless user experience
* Simple video recording functionality using existing front-end libraries (e.g., react-video- Recorder) and backend APIs (e.g., Firebase)
* Integration with a video hosting platform (e.g., Vimeo, YouTube) for secure video storage
* Limited analytics and user management features
* Single pricing tier with basic features

## Estimated Build Time
The initial MVP development should take approximately 8-12 weeks (2 months) for a well-communicative development team with relevant expertise in automation, frontend development, and backend APIs.

## Validation Steps
1. Create a minimal prototype (3-4 weeks) and test it with real users to gather feedback on usability, user experience, and product-market fit.
2. Develop a list of essential features for future iterations based on customer feedback and changing market conditions.

## Tech Stack
- Front-end: React, JavaScript, HTML, CSS
- Back-end: Node.js, Express.js
- Video Hosting: Firebase, Vimeo, or YouTube API
- Project Management: Asana, Trello, or Jira
