## Problem
Shopify store owners earning $10k-$100k/mo struggle to stay competitive with their pricing strategy, often resulting in lost sales and revenue. Current solutions are often clunky, expensive or unreliable.

## Target User
Shopify store owners earning $10k-$100k/mo who want to stay competitive and optimize their pricing strategy.

## Solution
The Competitor Price Monitor is a lightweight SaaS that monitors up to 20 competitor URLs and emails a daily price delta report to the user. This allows store owners to keep track of competitor pricing in real-time and make data-driven decisions to stay competitive.

## Revenue Model
The Competitor Price Monitor operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model. Store owners can subscribe to the service for a monthly fee, depending on the number of competitor URLs they want to monitor.

## MVP Format
The MVP format for the Competitor Price Monitor will be a simple web application that allows users to input up to 20 competitor URLs, schedule a daily report, and receive a daily email with price deltas. The MVP will not include any advanced features such as customization or integration with other tools.

## Estimated Build Time
4 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with Shopify store owners to validate the problem and identify key features.
2. Develop the MVP format and gather feedback from a small group of beta testers.
3. Conduct A/B testing to validate the effectiveness of the price delta report and other core features.
4. Continuously gather feedback and iterate on the product to improve user experience and engagement.

## Tech Stack
- Node.js with Express.js for the backend
- Next.js for the frontend
- SendGrid for email notifications
- A database (such as MongoDB or PostgreSQL) for storing competitor URLs and price data
- A task scheduler (such as Node.js's built-in scheduler or a library like Agenda) for scheduling daily reports
