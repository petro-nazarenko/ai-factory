## Problem
Remote engineering teams experience high turnover rates, which can be time-consuming and expensive to replace. Hiring, onboarding, and knowledge sharing processes are often manual, leading to inefficiencies and errors. Current market solutions often focus on skill assessments but neglect to address the entire hiring and onboarding lifecycle.

## Target User
Tech leaders in charge of remote engineering teams with high turnover rates. These individuals are concerned with maintaining team productivity, reducing costs, and improving the overall engineering experience.

## Solution
Workflow AutomationAI is a workflow automation tool that streamlines the hiring and onboarding process for remote cloud engineers. The platform includes the following features:

* Intelligent assessment tools to evaluate candidates' skills and fit for the team
* Automated onboarding workflows that integrate with existing HR systems
* Centralized knowledge sharing platform for knowledge retention and team collaboration
* Real-time analytics for optimizing hiring and onboarding processes

## Revenue Model
Workflow AutomationAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model. Clients can choose from three plans: Basic, Pro, and Enterprise, each offering varying levels of features and support.

## MVP Format
* Basic Features:
	+ Candidate assessment tools
	+ Automated onboarding workflows
	+ Knowledge sharing platform
* Integration with popular HR systems (e.g., BambooHR, Namely)
* Support for large teams (up to 50 engineers)

## Estimated Build Time
16 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct a survey of 100 tech leaders to gauge interest in a workflow automation tool for remote engineering teams.
2. Develop the initial MVP and conduct internal testing with a pilot team.
3. Gather feedback from pilot team members and tech leaders through user interviews and workshops.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React.js
- Backend: Node.js, Express.js
- Database: MongoDB
- Automation: Zapier, AWS Lambda
- Knowledge Sharing: Notion, Slack
