## Problem
New sales representatives in mid-sized B2B software companies often struggle with onboarding due to a lack of personalized training, inadequate coaching tools, and insufficient sales playbooks. This results in decreased sales performance, increased training costs, and a longer sales cycle.

## Target User
The primary target user is the Sales Operations Manager responsible for onboarding new sales representatives in mid-sized B2B software companies. Secondary users include new sales representatives and their direct managers.

## Solution
SDR AI is an AI-powered SDR onboarding platform that provides personalized sales playbooks, training modules, and coaching tools to maximize new rep performance. The platform utilizes machine learning algorithms to analyze sales data, customer interactions, and sales strategy to generate tailored onboarding plans for each new representative.

## Revenue Model
SDR AI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model. The pricing tiers are based on the number of licensed users and the level of customization required.

## MVP Format
Minimum Viable Product (MVP) format includes:

1. Basic Playbook Generator: AI-powered generator that creates a basic sales playbook for new representatives based on industry best practices.
2. Simple Training Module: A series of video-based training modules covering essential sales skills, such as prospecting, objection handling, and closing deals.
3. Limited Coaching Tools: Basic analytics and reporting capabilities to track new rep performance and identify areas for improvement.

## Estimated Build Time
Estimated build time is 3 months for MVP development and 6 months for the full-fledged product.

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with Sales Operations Managers and sales representatives in mid-sized B2B software companies to validate the problem and target user.
2. Develop a functional prototype and conduct usability testing with the target user group.
3. Gather feedback and iterate on the MVP to ensure it meets the needs of the target user.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React, Angular, or Vue.js for the user interface
- Backend: Node.js, Python, or Ruby on Rails for the API and data processing
- AI Engine: TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Microsoft Cognitive Toolkit for the machine learning algorithms
- Data Storage: MySQL, PostgreSQL, or MongoDB for data storage and analytics
