## Problem
Notion-using small business leaders and stakeholders struggle to track team workflow and productivity, leading to inefficiencies and missed opportunities for growth. Current solutions are manual and lack real-time insights, making it difficult to optimize team performance.

## Target User
Small business leaders and stakeholders who use Notion to manage their teams and workflows. Target user roles include:

* Team leaders
* Department heads
* Business owners
* Operations managers

## Solution
WorkplaceWise provides a seamless integration with Notion, offering advanced usage tracking, analytics, and reporting features. This enables business leaders to:

* Monitor team usage and productivity
* Identify areas of inefficiency
* Optimize workflows and processes
* Make data-driven decisions

## Revenue Model
Monthly SaaS subscription-based model, with tiered pricing to accommodate different business sizes and needs.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include:

* Notion template integration
* Real-time usage tracking
* Basic analytics and reporting
* Simple dashboard for business leaders to access key metrics

## Estimated Build Time
8 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with Notion-using small business leaders to validate the problem and gather feedback on the solution.
2. Develop a minimum viable product (MVP) for testing and iteration.
3. Pilot the MVP with a small group of beta testers to refine the solution and gather feedback.

## Tech Stack
- Notion API
- Server-side scripting (e.g., Node.js, Python)
- Front-end framework (e.g., React, Angular)
- Database solution (e.g., MongoDB, PostgreSQL)
- Cloud hosting (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud)
