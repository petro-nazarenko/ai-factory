## Problem
Small business owners and team leads struggle to set up and manage their Notion workspace, resulting in inefficiencies and delays in communicating and collaborating with their teams. Users spend a significant amount of time crafting custom workflows and templates from scratch, which can lead to version control issues, errors, and frustration.

## Target User
Notion-using small business owners and team leads who are looking for a more streamlined way to manage their workspace, set up workflows, and automate tasks.

## Solution
Onboard.ai provides pre-built, templated workspaces and workflow automation tools for cross-functional teams in Notion. Our solution includes a range of pre-built templates and workspaces that cater to different industries and use cases, as well as intuitive tools for customizing and automating workflows to fit specific business needs.

## Revenue Model
Monthly SaaS subscription with tiered pricing plans, offering scalability and flexibility to accommodate growing businesses and teams.

## MVP Format
Initial Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include:

* 10 pre-built templates for common use cases (e.g., project management, knowledge base, onboarding)
* Basic workflow automation capabilities (e.g., approval workflows, delegation, notification)
* Integrations with existing Notion features (e.g., databases, pages, blocks)
* User-friendly interface for easy navigation and customization

## Estimated Build Time
8 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct stakeholder interviews with Notion users and small business owners to validate the need for pre-built templates and automation tools.
2. Develop a working prototype of the Onboard.ai platform to test with a group of early adopters.
3. Collect feedback and iterate on the MVP to ensure it meets the needs and expectations of target users.

## Tech Stack
- Front-end: React, Notion API
- Back-end: Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB
- Database: MongoDB
- Integration: Notion API
