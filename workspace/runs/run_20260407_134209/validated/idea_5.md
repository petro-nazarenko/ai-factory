## Problem
Engineering Managers at expanding companies struggle to ensure that remote or distributed teams are properly on-boarded and integrated, leading to decreased productivity and increased downtime. Existing solutions often rely on manual processes, lack customization, or require extensive IT support.

## Target User
The primary target user is the Engineering Manager at a company with a remote or distributed team. This user is responsible for ensuring that new hires are properly on-boarded and integrated into the existing team.

## Solution
Pre-onboarding And Integration AI is a platform that streamlines the onboarding and integration process for remote or distributed teams. The platform leverages AI to automate tasks, customize workflows, and provide real-time support for knowledge transfer.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model for Pre-onboarding And Integration AI is a monthly SaaS subscription, with tiered pricing based on the size of the team and the level of customization required.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for Pre-onboarding And Integration AI will focus on the following features:

* Automated onboarding workflows for new team members
* Customizable knowledge transfer templates
* Real-time support for team collaboration

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP is 12 weeks, with a team of 2-3 developers and 1 project manager.

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct a thorough analysis of existing onboarding and integration solutions to identify areas for improvement.
2. Develop and test the automated onboarding workflow feature.
3. Launch a pilot program with a small group of engineering managers to gather feedback and iterate on the platform.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React.js
- Backend: Node.js with Express.js
- Database: MongoDB
- AI/ML: TensorFlow.js for custom workflows and knowledge transfer templates.
