## Problem
Remote companies face the challenge of navigating complex global regulations, increasing their risk of non-compliance and associated fines. Current solutions often rely on manual processes and are prone to human error.

## Target User
Business development managers at remote companies who are responsible for ensuring regulatory compliance.

## Solution
Regulator is an AI-powered platform that helps remote companies stay compliant with global regulations through risk assessments and alerts. The platform aggregates regulatory data, applies machine learning algorithms to identify potential compliance risks, and sends alerts to users for timely action.

## Revenue Model
Regulator operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering tiered pricing based on the size and complexity of the remote company's operations.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include:

1. Integration with a select group of global regulatory databases.
2. Basic risk assessment engine based on machine learning algorithms.
3. Email alert system for users.
4. Simple user interface for navigation and configuration.

## Estimated Build Time
12 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct market research with remote companies to validate demand and refine the product offering.
2. Develop a proof-of-concept prototype to test the platform's accuracy and feasibility.
3. Pilot with a small group of remote companies to gather feedback and iterate on the product.

## Tech Stack
- Python for backend development.
- Django for framework and API management.
- Elasticsearch for data aggregation and search.
- AWS Lambda for serverless architecture and scalability.
