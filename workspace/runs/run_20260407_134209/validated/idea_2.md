## Problem

Remote hiring managers for engineering companies struggle to find suitable site civil engineers with land development expertise. This leads to prolonged hiring processes and lower success rates in matching the right candidate to the job.

## Target User

- Remote hiring managers for engineering companies
- HR managers
- Recruitment agencies specializing in engineering roles

## Solution

The Job Matching AI platform is an automated system that filters resumes for site civil engineers with land development expertise. The platform uses natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning algorithms to analyze resumes and match them with job openings. The system can also provide job recommendations based on the candidate's skills and experience.

## Revenue Model

The platform operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model. The pricing will be tiered based on the number of users, with discounts for bulk sign-ups.

## MVP Format

- Initial platform development with basic resume filtering and job matching functionality
- Integration with a minimum of 5 popular job board platforms
- Limited user testing and feedback loop

## Estimated Build Time

- 12 weeks (approx. 3 months)

## Validation Steps

1. Conduct market research to validate demand and competition.
2. Develop a minimal viable product (MVP) and conduct user testing with a small group of remote hiring managers.
3. Gather feedback and iterate on the platform based on user reviews.
4. Launch the platform and monitor user adoption and revenue growth.

## Tech Stack

- Front-end: React.js, Node.js, and TypeScript
- Back-end: Express.js and MongoDB
- NLP and machine learning algorithms: Natural, SpaCy, and scikit-learn
- Integration with job boards: APIs and web scraping tools (e.g., Puppeteer)
