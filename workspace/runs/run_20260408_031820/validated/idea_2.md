## Problem
Law firms and immigration attorneys investing significant time manually filling and submitting government forms, leading to wasted resources and reduced focus on high-value tasks.

## Target User
Small to medium-sized law firms or individual immigration attorneys seeking to streamline and automate the form filling process for their clients.

## Solution
FormEase is a specialized AI-powered form filling and submission software designed for law firms and immigration attorneys. It automates the tedious process of filling government forms, reduces errors, and frees up time for more complex and high-value tasks.

## Revenue Model
FormEase operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering tiered pricing based on the number of users and submitted forms.

## MVP Format
Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will focus on a single government form type, allowing for seamless integration with existing law firm software and manual submission processes.

## Estimated Build Time
90-120 hours ( approx. 4-6 weeks with a dedicated development team)

## Validation Steps
1. Develop and test the AI-powered form filling feature for the selected government form type.
2. Gather feedback from 5-10 pilot law firms/immigration attorneys to validate user experience and value proposition.
3. Iterate on the MVP based on feedback and performance metrics.

## Tech Stack
- AI-powered form filling engine built on natural language processing (NLP)
- FormEase software built on Python, utilizing the Django framework.
- Integration with government forms databases and APIs.
- Web-based interface for user configuration and submission tracking.
- Subscription management and billing through Stripe API.
