## Problem
Shopify store owners earning $10k–$100k/mo face intense competition, making it challenging to stay up-to-date with price changes across their competitors' product offerings. Manually tracking prices can be time-consuming and may lead to missed opportunities or incorrect pricing strategies.

## Target User
The target user is a Shopify store owner with a monthly revenue of $10,000 to $100,000. These users are likely to have a established online presence, but may not have the resources or expertise to implement complex competitive monitoring solutions.

## Solution
A lightweight SaaS that monitors up to 20 competitor URLs and emails a daily price delta report. The solution will provide users with timely insights into price changes, enabling them to make informed pricing decisions and stay competitive in the market.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model will be based on a monthly SaaS subscription, with pricing tiers dependent on the number of competitor URLs being monitored. Additional features, such as historical price data or alerts for significant price changes, can be offered as premium add-ons.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will consist of the following features:
- User dashboard for adding and managing competitor URLs
- Daily price delta report via email
- Basic analytics for tracking price trends
- Integration with Shopify for seamless onboarding

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP is 12 weeks, with the following breakdown:
- Week 1-4: Research, planning, and design
- Week 5-8: Frontend development
- Week 9-12: Backend development, testing, and deployment

## Validation Steps
Validation of the solution will involve the following steps:
- Conducting surveys and interviews with potential users to gauge interest and gather feedback
- Creating a landing page to measure demand and collect email addresses from interested users
- Developing a functional prototype to test with a small group of users

## Tech Stack
The tech stack for the solution will include:
- Frontend: React, Material-UI
- Backend: Node.js, Express.js
- Database: MongoDB
- Web scraping: Puppeteer, Cheerio
- Email service: Sendgrid
- Integration: Shopify API, Zapier
