## Problem
B2B SaaS founders often struggle to collect high-quality testimonial videos from their customers, which can be a crucial aspect of marketing and sales efforts. The process of requesting, recording, and publishing testimonial videos can be cumbersome, involving multiple steps and requiring significant time and effort from both the founder and the customer.

## Target User
The target user for this solution is a B2B SaaS founder with 10-100 customers, who recognizes the value of testimonial videos in building trust and credibility with potential customers but lacks the resources or technical expertise to efficiently collect and utilize them.

## Solution
The solution is a one-page LinkAI that customers can open on their mobile devices to record a 60-second testimonial video without the need to install any app. This link can be easily shared via email, SMS, or other communication channels, making it convenient for customers to provide their feedback. The one-page link will include a brief introduction to the purpose of the testimonial, instructions on how to record, and a simple interface to start and stop the recording. Once recorded, the video will be automatically uploaded to a secure server, where it can be reviewed, edited (if necessary), and published by the SaaS founder.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model for this solution is a monthly SaaS subscription, where B2B SaaS founders pay a recurring fee to access the LinkAI tool. The subscription could offer different tiers, each with varying levels of features such as the number of testimonial videos that can be recorded per month, additional editing capabilities, or advanced analytics on video engagement.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include:
- A simple, user-friendly one-page link for customers to record their testimonial videos.
- Automatic video uploading to a secure server.
- Basic video editing capabilities for SaaS founders (e.g., trimming, adding a logo).
- A dashboard for SaaS founders to view, manage, and share recorded testimonials.

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP is approximately 12 weeks, assuming a team of 2-3 developers with expertise in front-end development, back-end development, and video processing. This timeframe includes planning, design, development, testing, and initial deployment.

## Validation Steps
To validate the solution, the following steps will be taken:
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with B2B SaaS founders to understand their current pain points and potential interest in the solution.
2. Develop a prototype and test it with a small group of early adopters to gather feedback on usability and functionality.
3. Analyze the response rate and quality of testimonials collected through the LinkAI compared to traditional methods.
4. Monitor subscription rates and customer retention to assess the solution's value proposition.

## Tech Stack
The tech stack for this solution will likely include:
- Front-end: React or Angular for building the one-page link and user interface.
- Back-end: Node.js with Express for handling video uploads, processing, and storing.
- Database: MongoDB for storing video metadata and customer information.
- Video Processing: Utilizing a cloud-based video processing service like AWS Elemental MediaConvert for encoding and editing videos.
- Authentication: Implementing OAuth or a similar protocol for secure authentication and authorization.
