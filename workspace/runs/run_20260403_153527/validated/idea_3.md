## Problem
B2B SaaS founders often struggle to collect high-quality testimonials from their customers, which are essential for building credibility and attracting new customers. Existing solutions may require customers to install apps, navigate complex recording software, or dedicate more time than they are willing to give.

## Target User
The target user for One-page LinkAI is a B2B SaaS founder with 10–100 customers, who values ease of use, simplicity, and efficiency in collecting testimonial videos from their customers. This user is likely to be interested in leveraging testimonials as a key component of their marketing strategy to build trust and demonstrate the value of their SaaS product.

## Solution
One-page LinkAI provides a simple, one-page link that customers can open on their mobile devices to record a 60-second testimonial video. The solution does not require any app installation, making it extremely user-friendly and accessible. The link can be customized with the SaaS company's branding and can include specific questions or prompts to guide the customer's testimonial. The recorded videos are then stored in a secure, easy-to-access dashboard for the SaaS founder to review, download, and use in their marketing efforts.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model for One-page LinkAI is based on a monthly SaaS subscription. Founders can choose from different tiers based on the number of customers they want to collect testimonials from, the level of customization they need, and the amount of video storage required. This model provides a predictable and scalable source of revenue as the customer base grows.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for One-page LinkAI will include:
- A customizable one-page link for recording testimonial videos.
- A simple dashboard for SaaS founders to access, review, and download recorded videos.
- Basic analytics to track the number of recordings and customer engagement.
- Integration with common marketing and CRM tools for seamless workflow integration.

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP version of One-page LinkAI is approximately 12 weeks, assuming a team of 2-3 full-stack developers, 1 designer, and 1 project manager. This timeline includes planning, design, development, testing, and initial deployment.

## Validation Steps
To validate the demand for One-page LinkAI, the following steps will be taken:
- Conduct surveys and interviews with B2B SaaS founders to understand their current pain points in collecting testimonials.
- Develop a landing page to gauge interest and collect email addresses from potential customers.
- Offer a free trial or a limited free version to a small group of early adopters to gather feedback and refine the product.

## Tech Stack
The tech stack for One-page LinkAI will likely include:
- Frontend: React or Vue.js for the one-page link and dashboard, ensuring a responsive design for mobile devices.
- Backend: Node.js with Express.js for the server-side logic, handling video uploads, and user authentication.
- Database: MongoDB for storing video recordings, customer information, and analytics data.
- Storage: AWS S3 for secure and scalable video storage.
- APIs: Integration with popular marketing and CRM tools via REST APIs or SDKs.
