## Problem
B2B SaaS founders struggle to collect high-quality, authentic customer testimonials that can be used to build trust and credibility with potential customers. Traditional methods of collecting testimonials, such as surveys or interviews, can be time-consuming and often result in low-quality or written testimonials that lack the emotional impact of video.

## Target User
The target user for One-page LinkAI is a B2B SaaS founder with 10-100 customers, who wants to collect authentic and high-quality customer testimonials to showcase on their website, social media, or sales materials. This user values ease of use, simplicity, and a seamless experience for both themselves and their customers.

## Solution
One-page LinkAI is a simple, one-page link that customers can open on their mobile devices to record a 60-second testimonial video. The link requires no app installation, making it easy and convenient for customers to provide a testimonial. The solution includes features such as:
* A customizable landing page with the company's branding and a clear call-to-action
* A guided recording process that prompts the customer to share their experience with the product or service
* Automatic video processing and hosting, with options for download or embedding on the company's website

## Revenue Model
The revenue model for One-page LinkAI is a monthly SaaS subscription, with pricing tiers based on the number of testimonials collected per month. Additional features, such as advanced analytics or custom branding, can be offered as upsells or add-ons.

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product (MVP) for One-page LinkAI will include:
* A basic, customizable landing page with a recording prompt
* A simple video recording and hosting feature
* Basic analytics and testimonial management

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP is 12-16 weeks, depending on the complexity of the features and the technology stack used.

## Validation Steps
To validate the solution, we will:
* Conduct customer interviews with B2B SaaS founders to understand their pain points and requirements for collecting customer testimonials
* Develop a prototype and test it with a small group of customers to gather feedback and iterate on the solution
* Launch a pilot program with a limited number of customers to test the scalability and effectiveness of the solution

## Tech Stack
The tech stack for One-page LinkAI will include:
* Front-end: React or Angular for building the customizable landing page
* Back-end: Node.js or Ruby on Rails for handling video recording, processing, and hosting
* Database: MongoDB or PostgreSQL for storing customer information and testimonial data
* Video hosting: AWS S3 or Google Cloud Storage for hosting and streaming testimonial videos
