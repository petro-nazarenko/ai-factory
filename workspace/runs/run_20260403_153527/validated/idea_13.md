## Problem
Freelance copywriters often spend a significant amount of time creating proposals for clients, which can be tedious and time-consuming. The process involves manually formatting and organizing the brief, researching the client's needs, and crafting a compelling proposal. This can lead to delays and take away from the time that could be spent on actual copywriting work.

## Target User
The target user for BriefGenie is a freelance copywriter with 3-10 clients. This user values their time and is looking for ways to streamline their workflow, increase productivity, and deliver high-quality proposals to their clients. They are likely familiar with technology and are willing to invest in tools that can help them achieve their goals.

## Solution
BriefGenie is a web app that uses AI to convert a raw brief into a polished proposal PDF in one click. The user simply pastes the brief into the app, and the AI engine analyzes the text, identifies key elements, and generates a professionally formatted proposal. The proposal includes all the necessary elements, such as an introduction, scope of work, timeline, and pricing.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model for BriefGenie is a monthly SaaS subscription. Users can choose from different plans, including a basic plan for solo freelancers and an advanced plan for larger teams or agencies. The subscription fee will be competitive with other SaaS solutions for freelancers and will offer a free trial or demo to allow users to test the app before committing to a paid plan.

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product (MVP) for BriefGenie will include the following features:
* A simple and intuitive user interface for pasting the brief and generating the proposal
* An AI engine that can analyze the brief and generate a basic proposal template
* A limited set of customization options, such as font and color schemes
* Integration with popular file storage services, such as Google Drive or Dropbox

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for BriefGenie will be approximately 12-16 weeks, depending on the complexity of the AI engine and the number of features included in the MVP. The development team will consist of 2-3 full-stack developers, a UI/UX designer, and a project manager.

## Validation Steps
To validate the idea for BriefGenie, we will take the following steps:
* Conduct surveys and interviews with freelance copywriters to understand their pain points and workflow
* Research existing solutions and identify gaps in the market
* Create a landing page to gauge interest and collect email addresses from potential users
* Develop a functional prototype to test with a small group of users and gather feedback

## Tech Stack
The tech stack for BriefGenie will include:
* Front-end: React or Angular for building the user interface
* Back-end: Node.js or Python for building the AI engine and handling API requests
* Database: MongoDB or PostgreSQL for storing user data and proposal templates
* AI/ML: Natural Language Processing (NLP) libraries, such as spaCy or Stanford CoreNLP, for analyzing the brief and generating the proposal
* Integration: APIs for integrating with file storage services and other third-party tools
