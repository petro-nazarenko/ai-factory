## Problem
B2B SaaS founders with a small to medium-sized customer base often struggle to collect high-quality testimonials from their customers. Existing solutions may require customers to install apps, navigate complex recording software, or dedicate more time than they are willing to give. This results in low response rates and a lack of social proof that could otherwise help attract new customers.

## Target User
The target user for One-page LinkAI is a B2B SaaS founder who has between 10 to 100 customers. This founder understands the importance of customer testimonials in marketing and sales but faces challenges in collecting them due to the friction involved in traditional recording methods.

## Solution
One-page LinkAI is a simple, mobile-optimized webpage that customers can access via a link sent to them by the SaaS founder. Upon opening the link, customers are guided through a brief, engaging process to record a 60-second video testimonial directly from their mobile device, without the need to install any application. The solution is designed to minimize friction and maximize the response rate by making the process as easy and quick as possible.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model for One-page LinkAI is based on a monthly SaaS subscription. SaaS founders pay a recurring fee to use the service, which allows them to send a certain number of testimonial requests to their customers each month. Additional features, such as custom branding, advanced analytics, or increased request limits, can be offered at higher tiers of subscription.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for One-page LinkAI will include:
- A customizable, mobile-optimized landing page where customers can record their video testimonials.
- A simple analytics dashboard for SaaS founders to view response rates and testimonial videos.
- Integration with popular email services to facilitate sending links to customers.
- Basic customization options for branding (e.g., logo, color scheme).

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP of One-page LinkAI is approximately 12 weeks. This timeframe assumes a small development team of 2-3 members, including a frontend developer, a backend developer, and a designer. The breakdown includes:
- Week 1-4: Planning, designing, and setting up the project infrastructure.
- Week 5-8: Developing the core features (landing page, recording functionality, analytics dashboard).
- Week 9-12: Testing, iterating, and deploying the MVP.

## Validation Steps
To validate the demand for One-page LinkAI, the following steps will be taken:
1. **Surveys and Interviews**: Conduct surveys and interviews with potential target users to understand their current pain points in collecting customer testimonials and their interest in a solution like One-page LinkAI.
2. **Landing Page Test**: Create a simple landing page to gauge interest and collect email addresses from potential customers interested in the solution.
3. **Pilot Program**: Offer a pilot program to a small group of SaaS founders to test the MVP and gather feedback for further development.

## Tech Stack
The tech stack for One-page LinkAI will include:
- **Frontend**: React or a similar JavaScript framework for building the customizable, mobile-optimized landing page.
- **Backend**: Node.js with Express for handling server-side logic, including user authentication, testimonial video processing, and analytics.
- **Database**: MongoDB or a similar NoSQL database for storing testimonial videos, customer information, and analytics data.
- **Cloud Services**: AWS or Google Cloud for hosting the application, storing videos, and leveraging services like email sending and authentication.
