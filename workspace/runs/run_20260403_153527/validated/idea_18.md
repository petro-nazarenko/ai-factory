## Problem
B2B SaaS founders with a small to medium-sized customer base often struggle to collect high-quality testimonials from their customers. Traditional methods, such as email requests or in-app prompts, can be cumbersome and yield low response rates. The lack of social proof can hinder the growth of their business, making it harder to attract new customers.

## Target User
The target user for One-page LinkAI is a B2B SaaS founder with 10-100 customers. This user values simplicity, ease of use, and effective ways to showcase customer satisfaction. They are likely familiar with the importance of social proof in converting leads into paying customers but may not have the resources or technical expertise to implement complex solutions.

## Solution
One-page LinkAI is a simple, mobile-first solution that allows customers to record a 60-second testimonial video with just one click. The link can be shared via email, SMS, or any other messaging platform, eliminating the need for app installation or complicated setup. The recorded video is then automatically uploaded to a centralized dashboard, where the SaaS founder can review, approve, and showcase the testimonials on their website or social media channels.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model for One-page LinkAI is a monthly SaaS subscription. The subscription fee will be tiered based on the number of customers and the features required. A basic plan could include a limited number of testimonial requests per month, while advanced plans could offer additional features such as custom branding, video editing, and integration with popular marketing automation tools.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for One-page LinkAI will include:
- A simple, one-page link that customers can click to record a 60-second testimonial video
- Automatic video uploading to a centralized dashboard
- Basic video review and approval functionality
- Integration with popular email services for easy link sharing

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP is approximately 12 weeks, assuming a team of two full-stack developers and one designer. The breakdown includes:
- Week 1-4: Planning, design, and frontend development
- Week 5-8: Backend development and API integration
- Week 9-12: Testing, debugging, and deployment

## Validation Steps
To validate the solution, the following steps will be taken:
- Conduct surveys and interviews with B2B SaaS founders to understand their current pain points and potential interest in One-page LinkAI
- Develop a landing page to gauge interest and collect email addresses from potential customers
- Launch a pilot program with a small group of early adopters to test the MVP and gather feedback

## Tech Stack
The tech stack for One-page LinkAI will include:
- Frontend: React or Vue.js for building the one-page link and dashboard
- Backend: Node.js with Express.js for handling API requests and video uploads
- Database: MongoDB or PostgreSQL for storing customer information and testimonial videos
- Video Recording: Utilize WebRTC or a similar technology for in-browser video recording
- Integration: API integrations with popular email services and marketing automation tools
