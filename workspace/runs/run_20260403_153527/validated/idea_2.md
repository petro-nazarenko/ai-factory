## Problem
Shopify store owners earning $10k–$100k/mo struggle to stay competitive due to the lack of timely and accurate pricing information about their competitors. Manual monitoring of competitor prices is time-consuming and may lead to lost sales or reduced profit margins.

## Target User
The target user for this solution is a Shopify store owner who earns between $10,000 and $100,000 per month. This user is likely to be interested in staying competitive in their market and is willing to invest in tools that can help them achieve this goal.

## Solution
A lightweight SaaS that monitors up to 20 competitor URLs and emails a daily price delta report. This solution will provide Shopify store owners with timely and accurate information about their competitors' prices, allowing them to make informed decisions about their own pricing strategy.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model for this solution is a monthly SaaS subscription. Users will be charged a recurring fee to access the competitor price monitoring and reporting features.

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product (MVP) for this solution will include the following features:
- Competitor URL monitoring (up to 20 URLs)
- Daily price delta report via email
- User dashboard for managing competitor URLs and viewing reports
- Integration with Shopify for easy setup and authentication

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for this solution is 12-16 weeks, assuming a team of 2-3 developers and 1 designer. This includes time for planning, designing, developing, testing, and deploying the solution.

## Validation Steps
To validate this solution, the following steps will be taken:
- Conduct surveys and interviews with Shopify store owners to confirm the need for a competitor price monitoring solution
- Analyze existing solutions in the market to identify gaps and opportunities
- Develop a prototype and test it with a small group of users to gather feedback and iterate on the solution

## Tech Stack
The tech stack for this solution will include:
- Frontend: React or Angular for building the user dashboard
- Backend: Node.js or Python for building the API and handling competitor URL monitoring and reporting
- Database: MySQL or MongoDB for storing competitor URL and pricing data
- Integration: Shopify API for integrating with Shopify stores
- Email service: Sendgrid or Mailgun for sending daily price delta reports via email
