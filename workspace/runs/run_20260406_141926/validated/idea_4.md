## Problem
Meeting notes management can be a tedious and disorganized process for teams using Notion for collaboration. Without a clear system, important notes and action items can fall through the cracks, leading to misunderstandings and lost productivity.

## Target User
Engineer leads at mid-sized tech companies who use Notion as their primary collaboration tool.

## Solution
MeetEcho is a meeting notes management system that integrates with Notion to provide a centralized platform for teams to share, organize, and take action on meeting notes. MeetEcho seamlessly syncs with Notion, allowing teams to easily capture notes, assign action items, and track progress in real-time.

## Revenue Model
MeetEcho operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, providing teams with access to premium features and support.

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product will include:

* Integration with Notion
* Meeting note capture and syncing
* Action item assignment and tracking
* Real-time progress updates

## Estimated Build Time
Estimated development time for the MVP is 12 weeks, with a focused team of 2-3 engineers and 1 project manager.

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct user interviews with engineer leads at mid-sized tech companies to validate the problem and identify key requirements.
2. Develop a prototype and conduct usability testing to ensure a seamless user experience.
3. Gather feedback from early adopters and iterate on the product to ensure it meets their needs.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React.js for meeting note capture and syncing
- Backend: Node.js with Express.js for API integration with Notion
- Database: MongoDB for storing meeting notes and action items
- Authentication: OAuth 2.0 for secure integration with Notion
- CI/CD: Jenkins for continuous integration and deployment
- Cloud: AWS for hosting and scalability
