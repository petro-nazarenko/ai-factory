## Problem
Marketing managers and team leads at remote-first companies struggle to foster a sense of community, encourage knowledge sharing, and build meaningful relationships among team members. Without face-to-face interactions, it's challenging to develop a cohesive company culture, leading to feelings of isolation and disconnection among team members.

## Target User
The primary target user is marketing managers and team leads working in remote-first companies, who use Notion for team collaboration and are looking for a solution to address the challenges of remote work culture.

## Solution
Hubhive is a social networking platform designed specifically for remote teams, aiming to replicate the benefits of in-person interactions. It features a centralized hub for team members to connect, share knowledge, and build relationships through:

- Community forums for discussions and idea exchange
- Personalized profiles for showcasing team member skills and expertise
- Virtual event planning and scheduling
- Integration with Notion to streamline communication and collaboration

## Revenue Model
Hubhive operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering tiered pricing plans based on team size:

- Basic: $10/user/month (for teams of 1-5 members)
- Premium: $20/user/month (for teams of 6-15 members)
- Enterprise: Custom pricing for large teams and enterprises

## MVP Format
To begin, the MVP will include:

- Core community features: user profiles, forums, and event planning
- Notion integration: seamless collaboration and data sharing
- Basic analytics and reporting for team administrators

## Estimated Build Time
12 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct user research through surveys and interviews to validate the target user pain points and needs.
2. Develop a minimum viable product (MVP) to gather feedback from a small pilot group of remote teams.
3. Analyze usage data and feedback to inform future development and feature enhancements.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React
- Backend: Node.js, Express.js
- Database: MongoDB
- Integration: Notion API
- Deployment: Cloud-based infrastructure with auto-scaling and load balancing
- CI/CD: Jenkins, Docker, and Kubernetes for automated testing, deployment, and DevOps.
