## Problem

Many product managers face challenges in implementing and maximizing the usage of Notion for project management within their teams. This includes difficulties in onboarding, using Notion's full potential, and tracking adoption.

## Target User

Product managers at companies using Notion for project management, who struggle with:

* Inadequate training and onboarding for their teams
* Low adoption rates of Notion's features
* Difficulty in measuring and tracking Notion usage
* Limited insights into team's workflow and process efficiency

## Solution

NotionFit is a product adoption platform designed to help teams get the most out of Notion. It provides a comprehensive solution with the following key features:

* **Training and Onboarding**: Guided video tutorials, interactive quizzes, and hands-on exercises to help teams learn Notion features and workflows.
* **Analytics and Reporting**: Customizable dashboards to track team's Notion usage, identify bottlenecks, and measure progress.
* **Community Support**: Access to a private community forum where users can ask questions, share ideas, and get support from product experts.

## Revenue Model

NotionFit operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model. The pricing tiered based on the number of users:

* **Starter**: $9/user/month (billed annually) - Access to basic training and analytics features
* **Pro**: $19/user/month (billed annually) - Access to advanced analytics, community support, and priority support
* **Business**: Custom pricing for large organizations with complex needs

## MVP Format

MVP will include the following core features:

* Basic training and onboarding materials
* Basic analytics and reporting capabilities
* Private community support forum

## Estimated Build Time

6 weeks

## Validation Steps

1. Conduct surveys and interviews with product managers from similar organizations.
2. Analyze existing Notion adoption platforms and identify gaps.
3. Create a basic prototype and test it with a small group of users.

## Tech Stack

* **Frontend**: React.js and Tailwind CSS
* **Backend**: Node.js and Express.js
* **Database**: MongoDB
* **Integration**: Notion API for seamless integration
* **Analytics**: Google Analytics and Mixpanel for user tracking and analysis
* **Community Platform**: Discord API for community support and discussions
