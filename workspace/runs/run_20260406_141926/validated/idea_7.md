## Problem
Engineering firms struggle to attract top talent due to tedious and time-consuming job description creation. HR managers spend hours crafting job postings to meet company and location-specific requirements, hindering their ability to efficiently fill senior engineering positions.

## Target User
HR Managers at mid-sized to large engineering firms with multiple locations, responsible for talent acquisition and HR strategies.

## Solution
JobDescriptionAI uses natural language processing and machine learning to generate AI-powered, location-specific job descriptions for senior engineering roles, empowering HR managers to easily publish job postings on multiple platforms, streamline their hiring process, and attract top engineering talent.

## Revenue Model
Monthly SaaS subscription model, providing access to the JobDescriptionAI platform and premium features. Businesses can choose from tiered pricing plans, depending on their organizational size and needs.

## MVP Format
Minimum Viable Product (MVP):

* AI-powered job description generator for senior engineering roles
* Location-specific job description creation
* Capability to publish job postings on multiple job boards
* Basic analytics and reporting

## Estimated Build Time
12 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct usability testing with HR managers from target engineering firms
2. Gather feedback on AI-generated job descriptions through A/B testing
3. Validate subscription model with pilot customers

## Tech Stack
- Front-end: React, Tailwind CSS
- Back-end: Node.js, Express, Postgres
- AI/ML: TensorFlow, NLTK
- Job board integrations: APIs for LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor
