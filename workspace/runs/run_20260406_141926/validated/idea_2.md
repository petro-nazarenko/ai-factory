## Problem

Engineering recruiters and HR managers at media companies struggle to showcase their company's unique culture, values, and mission to attract top engineering talent. Existing job posting platforms are often too generic and fail to represent the brand's personality, resulting in a lack of engagement from potential candidates.

## Target User

Engineering recruiters or HR managers at media companies with 500+ employees, who are responsible for managing the company's engineering talent acquisition process.

## Solution

Bespoke JobAI is a customizable job posting platform that allows unique media companies to showcase their culture, values, and mission to attract top engineering talent. The platform features:

1. Branding and styling options to match the company's website and social media presence.
2. Customizable job descriptions and requirements to reflect the company's specific needs.
3. Integration with popular job board and social media platforms.
4. Analytics and insights to track candidate engagement and application rates.
5. Content creation templates to help companies showcase their culture and values.

## Revenue Model

Monthly SaaS subscription based on the number of job postings created per month:

* 1-5 job postings: $500/month
* 6-10 job postings: $1,000/month
* 11+ job postings: custom quote

## MVP Format

The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include the following features:

1. Customizable job posting templates
2. Basic analytics and insights
3. Integration with 2-3 popular job boards

## Estimated Build Time

Assuming a development team of 3-5 people, the estimated build time for the MVP is 12-16 weeks.

## Validation Steps

1. Conduct surveys with 10-15 engineering recruiters and HR managers at media companies to validate the problem and gather feedback on the solution.
2. Develop a prototype of the platform and conduct usability testing with 5-10 users to validate the design and user experience.
3. Launch a pilot program with 3-5 media companies to test the platform in a real-world setting.

## Tech Stack

* Front-end: React, CSS-in-JS (e.g. styled-components)
* Back-end: Node.js, Express
* Database: MongoDB or PostgreSQL
* Integration tools: Zapier, API connectors (e.g. Indeed, LinkedIn)
