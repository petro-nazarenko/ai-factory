## Problem
New Sales Development Representatives struggle to integrate into the remote sales team, leading to decreased performance, increased time-to-revenue, and higher turnover rates.

## Target User
HR Managers in charge of onboarding Sales Development Representatives in remote companies.

## Solution
OnboardSync is an automated, customizable onboarding platform that introduces new Sales hires to company policies, team members, and tools, ensuring seamless integration into the remote sales team.

## Revenue Model
OnboardSync operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering tiered pricing based on the number of users, features, and support level.

## MVP Format
MVP (Minimum Viable Product) format includes:

1. Automated onboarding workflow with customizable welcome emails.
2. Integration with popular sales tools (e.g., CRM, sales engagement platforms).
3. Access to an onboarding playbook with templated content and training resources.
4. Basic analytics and reporting on onboarding completion and effectiveness.

## Estimated Build Time
10 weeks (2.5 months)

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct a pilot onboarding of 5-10 new Sales Development Representatives to test the platform's effectiveness.
2. Collect feedback from participants and stakeholders to refine the platform.
3. Analyze key onboarding metrics (e.g., completion rates, time-to-revenue) to measure success.
4. Compare results to companies using manual onboarding processes to demonstrate the value of OnboardSync.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React.js
- Backend: Node.js, Express
- Database: MongoDB
- Integration APIs: CRM, sales engagement platforms
- Automation tools: Zapier, Automation Anywhere
- Hosting: AWS
- Security: Google Cloud IAM, AWS Cognito
