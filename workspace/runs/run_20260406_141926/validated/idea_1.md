## Problem
Media and educational companies struggle to find the right engineering talent for their unique projects. Traditional recruitment agencies and job boards often lead to unqualified candidates and wasted resources.

## Target User
HR managers at media and educational companies, such as PBS, who need to fill engineering positions.

## Solution
Our AI-powered job matching platform connects these companies with a curated pool of suitable candidates. By leveraging machine learning algorithms and a vast candidate database, we ensure that job openings are matched with the most qualified and interested candidates.

## Revenue Model
Our revenue model is based on a monthly SaaS subscription, where companies pay a flat fee to access premium features and a vast candidate network.

## MVP Format
MVP (Minimum Viable Product) will include:

* Integration with popular job boards and social media platforms for candidate sourcing
* Simple AI-powered candidate matching algorithm
* Basic candidate analytics and reporting
* Limited user accounts for HR teams

## Estimated Build Time
12 weeks (approx. 600 hours)

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct surveys with HR managers at media and educational companies
2. Analyze industry benchmarks and competitive pricing strategies
3. Conduct A/B testing with different AI-powered matching algorithms
4. Gather feedback from early adopters and refine the platform accordingly

## Tech Stack
- Language: Python
- Framework: Flask or Django for backend development
- Database: PostgreSQL or MongoDB for scalable data storage
- AI/ML library: TensorFlow or scikit-learn for machine learning algorithms
- Frontend: React or Angular for building the web application
