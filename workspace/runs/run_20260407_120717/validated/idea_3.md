## Problem
Senior hiring managers at startups and scale-ups face challenges in attracting top talent, often due to high competition, inefficient traditional recruitment methods, and the increasing adoption of decentralized technologies among job seekers. Decentralized job boards and talent pipelines can help address these issues by providing a transparent and community-driven platform for recruitment.

## Target User
Senior hiring managers at startups and scale-ups interested in decentralized technologies, looking to streamline their recruitment processes and attract top talent.

## Solution
A Decentralized JobAI is a decentralized job board and talent pipeline platform that enables seamless communication and collaboration between hiring managers, job seekers, and trusted community members. This platform facilitates the discovery of top talent, fosters community engagement, and ensures the authenticity of job listings and candidate profiles.

## Revenue Model
The platform generates revenue through a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering various tiered pricing plans catering to the needs of different-sized startups and scale-ups.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for the Decentralized JobAI platform consists of:

1. Decentralized job board with customizable job listings and automated matching.
2. Talent pipeline management system for streamline workflow and collaboration.
3. Integration with Web3 identity verification for trusted community members.
4. Smart contract-based subscription management and payment processing.

## Estimated Build Time
Estimated build time: 24 weeks (6 months)

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct market research and surveys to gauge interest in decentralized job boards.
2. Develop and deploy a proof-of-concept for the decentralized job board and talent pipeline.
3. Conduct usability testing and gather feedback from hiring managers and job seekers.
4. Engage with the community and maintain open communication channels.

## Tech Stack
- Framework: Ethereum (Smart Contracts)
- Programming language: Solidity
- Client-side integration: Web3.js
- Front-end framework: React
- Database: Decentralized storage solutions (e.g., IPFS)
