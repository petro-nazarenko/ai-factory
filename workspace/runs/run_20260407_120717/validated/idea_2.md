## Problem
Companies with numerous third-party vendors face challenges in managing risks and ensuring compliance. Manual assessments are time-consuming, prone to errors, and fail to provide actionable insights.

## Target User
Risk Managers at companies with 500+ employees and 100+ vendors who require efficient and accurate risk assessments.

## Solution
Third-party Vendor RiskAI is an AI-powered platform for vendor risk assessment and management. It centralizes vendor data, provides real-time risk scores, and offers tailored recommendations for mitigation.

## Revenue Model
Monthly SaaS subscription-based model, offering varying tiers based on the number of vendors and features required.

## MVP Format
Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include:

* Data ingestion from existing vendor databases
* AI-powered risk scoring based on industry standards and best practices
* Initial risk assessment and scoring for 50 high-risk vendors
* Basic reporting and analytics capabilities

## Estimated Build Time
12-16 weeks for the MVP, with ongoing development and enhancement of the platform based on user feedback and market trends.

## Validation Steps
1. Develop a comprehensive vendor risk assessment questionnaire.
2. Integrate the survey with the MVP platform.
3. Conduct a pilot with 50 high-risk vendors to refine the AI model.
4. Gather feedback from pilot participants and adjust the platform accordingly.

## Tech Stack
- Cloud-based infrastructure (AWS or Azure)
- Machine learning libraries (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch) for AI-powered risk scoring
- API integration with popular vendor databases (e.g., Nav, Crunchbase)
- Front-end framework (e.g., React, Angular) for user interface
- Database management system (e.g., MySQL, PostgreSQL) for vendor data storage
