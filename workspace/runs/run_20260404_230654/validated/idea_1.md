## Problem
Mid-sized companies struggle to find and hire top engineering talent, often leading to lengthy hiring processes and mediocre candidate matches. This problem extends to engineers themselves, who may feel undervalued or unchallenged in their roles.

## Target User
Tech hiring managers at mid-sized companies, responsible for finding and hiring top engineers.

## Solution
Our AI Job Match Platform will utilize AI-powered matching algorithms to connect top engineers with job openings that perfectly align with their interests and skills. By providing a curated and matched set of opportunities, we aim to streamline the hiring process for both companies and engineers.

## Revenue Model
The platform will operate on a monthly SaaS subscription model, charging hiring managers and companies for access to our premium features and pool of top engineers.

## MVP Format
MVP will include the following features:

1. **Job Posting**: Companies can post job openings with detailed requirements and descriptions.
2. **Engineer Profiler**: A questionnaire for engineers to input their skills, interests, and preferences.
3. **AI Matching Algorithm**: Matches job openings with engineers based on their profiles.
4. **Personalized Matching Portal**: Engineers will receive a personalized portal to browse and apply to matched job openings.

## Estimated Build Time
16 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct surveys among tech hiring managers and engineers to validate the pain points and requirements of the platform.
2. Develop a basic version of the platform with a limited set of features and test it with a small group of pilot users.
3. Collect feedback and iterate on the platform to refine its features and user experience.

## Tech Stack
- Backend: Node.js with Express.js for API and microservices
- Frontend: React.js for the web application
- Database: MongoDB for data storage
- AI Matching Algorithm: TensorFlow.js for machine learning model development and deployment.
