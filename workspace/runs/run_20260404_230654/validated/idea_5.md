## Problem
Recruiters and hiring managers in the scientific computing and fintech industries struggle to find the best technical talent. The traditional interview process is often inefficient and biased towards candidates with strong communication skills but less technical expertise. The lack of a standardized evaluation framework and the high costs of conducting in-person interviews hinder the discovery of the most suitable candidates.

## Target User
Recruiters or hiring managers at companies in the scientific computing or fintech industry who require a reliable and efficient video interview platform for assessing technical skills.

## Solution
Video Interview AI is a cutting-edge video interview platform that integrates AI-driven tools to streamline the interview process for technical roles. The platform enables real-time live coding challenges, immediate feedback, and comprehensive evaluation. This platform optimizes the search for top talent by assessing a candidate's technical abilities and making data-driven decisions.

## Revenue Model
Video Interview AI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering various tiered pricing plans that accommodate different business needs and requirements.

## MVP Format
The initial Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will comprise the following features:
- Live video conferencing for real-time communication
- Integrated coding environment for live coding challenges
- AI-driven feedback tool for assessing candidate performance
- Customizable question library for evaluating technical skills
- Basic analytics and reporting for tracking interview performance

## Estimated Build Time
Estimated build time for the MVP is approximately 6-8 months, depending on the complexity of individual features and the resources allocated to the project.

## Validation Steps
1. Pilot program with at least 20 companies in the scientific computing and fintech industries to assess the platform's effectiveness.
2. Conduct detailed user feedback sessions to refine and improve the platform.
3. Perform A/B testing to analyze the impact of AI-driven feedback on interview outcomes.
4. Analyze platform adoption rates and interview completion rates to evaluate viability.

## Tech Stack
- Front-end: 
  - WebRTC for live video conferencing
  - CodeMirror for live coding environment
  - React for UI components
- Back-end: 
  - Node.js for server-side logic
  - Express.js for API implementation
  - MongoDB for database management
- AI:
  - TensorFlow.js for machine learning models
  - Natural language processing for AI-driven feedback analysis
