## Problem
Current recruiting processes for tech companies are time-consuming, expensive, and prone to bias. Manual skills assessments and interviews often fail to accurately identify the best candidates.

## Target User
Recruiters at tech companies, particularly those in the engineering and development departments.

## Solution
AI. Target is a cloud-based platform that revolutionizes the way tech companies find and hire top engineering talent. It provides AI-powered coding challenges and simulated interview questions that accurately assess candidates' skills and fit for the company's specific needs.

## Revenue Model
AI. Target operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model. Companies can choose from various plans based on their hiring volume and customization requirements. The revenue structure is as follows:

* Basic Plan: $500/month (limited features, suitable for small-scale hiring)
* Standard Plan: $2,000/month (includes advanced features, suitable for mid-sized companies)
* Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing (includes full customization, support, and onboarding)

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include the following core features:

* Integration with popular job boards and applicant tracking systems (ATS)
* AI-powered coding challenges and interview questions
* Automated assessment and scoring
* Basic analytics and reporting
* Limited customization options

## Estimated Build Time
24-30 weeks (6-8 months) to develop the MVP, considering the complexity of the platform and the need for iterative development.

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct user research and gather feedback from recruiters and engineering leaders in the tech industry.
2. Develop a proof-of-concept (POC) to validate the AI-powered coding challenges and simulated interview questions.
3. Create a working MVP to test with a small group of pilot customers.
4. Gather feedback from pilot customers and iterate on the platform.

## Tech Stack
- Front-end: React.js, Redux
- Back-end: Node.js, Express.js
- Database: MongoDB
- AI/ML: TensorFlow.js, Amazon Rekognition
- Integration: RESTful API, Webhooks
- Deploy: AWS Lambda, Amazon API Gateway
