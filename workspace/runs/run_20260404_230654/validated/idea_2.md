## Problem
As a tech hiring manager at a mid-sized company, manually posting job openings on multiple platforms can be time-consuming and inefficient. Current solutions often require separate sign-ups, login credentials, and tracking of applicants across numerous platforms.

## Target User
- Tech hiring manager at a mid-sized company
- HR specialists dealing with recruitment processes
- Recruitment agencies looking to streamline their workflow

## Solution
All-in-one JobAI is an all-in-one job posting platform that seamlessly integrates with popular job boards (e.g. LinkedIn, Glassdoor) and allows companies to:

- Easily post job openings across multiple platforms from a single dashboard
- Track applicants in one place, with real-time updates and notifications
- Streamline the hiring process with features such as applicant filtering, screening, and scheduling

## Revenue Model
The solution operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering various tiers to cater to different business needs. Revenue is generated through:

- Monthly subscription fees
- Optional premium features and support packages

## MVP Format
Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include:

- Integration with two major job boards (e.g. LinkedIn, Glassdoor)
- Basic user management and job posting functionality
- Minimal applicant tracking features (e.g. basic filtering)

## Estimated Build Time
Estimated development time for MVP: 8 weeks (~160 hours)
- Development: 60% (~96 hours)
- Testing and QA: 20% (~32 hours)
- Deployment and rollout: 20% (~32 hours)

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with tech hiring managers to gauge demand and identify key features.
2. Develop a basic prototype to validate the user flow and integrate with a single job board.
3. Analyze user engagement and feedback on the MVP to inform further development and iteration.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React.js with Redux state management
- Backend: Node.js with Express.js and MongoDB
- API Integrations: LinkedIn and Glassdoor APIs for job posting and applicant data retrieval
- Deployment: Cloud-based hosting on AWS or Google Cloud with autoscaling and load balancing
