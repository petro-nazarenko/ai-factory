## Problem
E-commerce site owners and online platform operators with limited resources struggle to maintain website security, often leading to vulnerabilities, downtime, and devastating losses due to cyber threats and data breaches.

## Target User
Small to medium-sized e-commerce sites (1-5 employees), online marketplaces, and digital platforms seeking automated security and maintenance solutions to protect their online presence.

## Solution
SiteShield is a Software as a Service (SaaS) tool that detects and alerts businesses to potential security threats, updates, and maintenance issues on their websites. Automated scans provide a secure environment, ensuring compliance with industry regulations and minimizing the risk of data breaches.

## Revenue Model
SiteShield operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering tiered pricing for varying levels of service, including Standard, Pro, and Enterprise plans. Pricing will be based on the number of websites, frequency of scans, and access to premium features.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) version of SiteShield will include:

1. Automated website security scans for common vulnerabilities and threats.
2. Customizable alerting system for notified users via email, SMS, or push notifications.
3. Integration with popular e-commerce platforms (e.g., Shopify, WooCommerce).
4. Multi-level user permissions for granular access control.

## Estimated Build Time
12 weeks (approx. 240 business hours)

## Validation Steps
1. Develop and test the core security scan functionality.
2. Integrate with a minimum of two popular e-commerce platforms.
3. Conduct a small-scale pilot with a group of target users to gather feedback.
4. Gather and analyze user feedback to inform the product roadmap.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React.js with a Material-UI theme
- Backend: Node.js with Express.js framework
- Database: PostgreSQL for storing scan results and user data
- Integration: Official APIs for supported e-commerce platforms
- Security: OWASP ZAP for web application scanning, SSL/TLS certificates for encrypted communication
