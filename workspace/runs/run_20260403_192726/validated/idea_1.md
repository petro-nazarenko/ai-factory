## Problem
Engineering hiring managers at large public media organizations often struggle to find qualified engineers for short-term or project-based work, leading to delays and increased costs. The traditional hiring process can be lengthy and may not provide the flexibility needed for dynamic project requirements.

## Target User
The target user is an engineering hiring manager at a large public media organization, responsible for overseeing the technical aspects of media production, software development, and infrastructure management. They require a reliable and efficient way to connect with skilled engineers who can work on a flexible, project-based basis.

## Solution
FlexiEngage is a platform that connects companies with pre-vetted engineers for flexible, project-based work. The platform allows hiring managers to post project requirements, receive bids from qualified engineers, and manage the entire workflow from a single interface. Engineers, on the other hand, can create profiles, showcase their skills, and bid on projects that match their expertise.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model for FlexiEngage is based on a monthly SaaS subscription, where companies pay a recurring fee to access the platform and connect with pre-vetted engineers. The subscription fee can be tiered based on the number of projects, engineers, or features required.

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product (MVP) for FlexiEngage would include the following features:
- Company profile creation and project posting
- Engineer profile creation and project bidding
- Basic project management and workflow tools
- Payment processing and transaction management

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP version of FlexiEngage is approximately 400 hours, assuming a team of 4-6 developers, designers, and project managers working full-time.

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct market research to validate the demand for a flexible, project-based engineering platform.
2. Interview potential customers (hiring managers) to gather feedback on the proposed solution and features.
3. Develop a prototype and test it with a small group of users to gather feedback and iterate on the design.
4. Partner with a few engineering teams to test the platform and refine the workflow.

## Tech Stack
- Front-end framework: React or Angular
- Back-end framework: Node.js or Django
- Database management: MySQL or MongoDB
- Payment gateway integration: Stripe or PayPal
- Project management tools: Trello or Asana integration
