## Problem
Freelance builders and contractors struggle to efficiently estimate and bid on projects using outdated templates and manual data entry, leading to lost opportunities and missed deadlines.

## Target User
Freelance builders and contractors with 1-5 clients, using job boards to find new projects.

## Solution
Estimate offers a customizable template library and AI-assisted proposal generation, enabling freelancers to quickly and professionally estimate and bid on projects.

## Revenue Model
Monthly SaaS subscription, with tiered pricing for users based on the number of proposals generated per month.

## MVP Format
- Pre-built templates for common construction projects
- AI-powered proposal generator using natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning (ML)
- Integrations with popular job boards and CRM systems
- User dashboard for tracking proposal performance

## Estimated Build Time
6 months

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct user interviews with freelance builders and contractors to validate the problem and gather feedback on the estimated solution.
2. Develop a minimum viable product (MVP) with a limited set of templates and AI capabilities, and launch a beta test with a small group of users.
3. Gather feedback from beta testers and iterate on the MVP to improve its features and usability.
4. Conduct A/B testing on different pricing tiers and subscription models to determine the most effective revenue strategy.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React.js, Webpack
- Backend: Node.js, Express.js
- AI Engine: TensorFlow.js, NLP library (e.g. Stanford CoreNLP)
- Database: PostgreSQL
- Integration APIs: APIs for popular job boards and CRM systems
- User Authentication: OAuth, JWT
