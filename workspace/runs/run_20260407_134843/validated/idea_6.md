## Problem
Freelance builders and contractors struggle with manual data entry, inefficient proposal tracking, and disjointed client communication, leading to decreased productivity and client satisfaction.

## Target User
Freelance builders and contractors with 1-5 clients using job boards, primarily focusing on residential or small commercial projects.

## Solution
Estimeasy Offers AnAI is a user-friendly, all-in-one platform that automates key tasks for freelance builders and contractors, including:

* Automated data import from job boards and other sources
* Proposal tracking, allowing for easy organization and management of client projects
* Client communication features, enabling seamless interaction and updates

## Revenue Model
Estimeasy Offers AnAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, providing tiered pricing plans to accommodate various client needs and business sizes.

## MVP Format
Minimum Viable Product (MVP) format will include:

* Automated data import from major job boards
* Basic proposal tracking and client communication features
* Integration with essential calendar and messaging services

## Estimated Build Time
60-80 hours

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with target users to gather feedback and assess interest in Estimeasy Offers AnAI.
2. Develop a functional prototype and test its usability and effectiveness.
3. Gather user feedback and iterate the platform accordingly.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend development: ReactJS
- Backend development: Node.js with Express
- Database management: MongoDB
- Automation: Zapier and/or custom-built integrations
- Calendar and messaging integration: Google Calendar, Slack or Microsoft Teams
