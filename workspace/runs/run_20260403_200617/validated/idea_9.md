## Problem
Small to medium-sized websites often struggle with maintaining their online presence due to limited resources and technical expertise. Manual updates, backups, and security checks can be time-consuming, increasing the risk of errors and downtime.

## Target User
Remote company founders and operations managers with 1-10 team members, responsible for maintaining their company's website.

## Solution
SiteSavvy is a website maintenance automation platform that leverages AI to streamline updates, backups, and security checks. Our platform continuously monitors the website's performance, identifies issues, and automates necessary fixes to minimize downtime and errors.

## Revenue Model
SiteSavvy operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering customizable plans based on the size and complexity of the client's website.

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product (MVP) for SiteSavvy will include the following features:

1. Automated website updates: AI-driven updates for content management systems (CMS) and plugins.
2. Scheduled backups: Regular backups of website files and databases.
3. Security checks: Automated scans for security threats and vulnerabilities.
4. Real-time monitoring: Continuous monitoring of website performance and issues.

## Estimated Build Time
SiteSavvy requires an estimated 20-30 weeks of development, considering the development of AI-driven features and integration with various CMS platforms.

## Validation Steps
1. Survey remote company founders and operations managers to understand their current pain points and challenges with website maintenance.
2. Develop a prototype of SiteSavvy and test it with a small group of beta users.
3. Conduct user acceptance testing (UAT) to gather feedback and iterate on the platform's features.

## Tech Stack
- Programming languages: Python, JavaScript
- AI/ML frameworks: TensorFlow, Scikit-learn
- CMS platforms: WordPress, Joomla, Drupal
- Database: PostgreSQL
- Cloud platform: AWS
- Frontend framework: React
