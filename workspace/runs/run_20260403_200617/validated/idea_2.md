## Problem
Corporations experience difficulties in maintaining up-to-date documentation of team roles, responsibilities, and knowledge bases, leading to inefficiencies, miscommunication, and errors.

## Target User
The target user is an IT manager or Operations lead who is responsible for maintaining corporate documentation and ensuring team members have access to the most current information.

## Solution
The AI-Driven Corporate Wiki Platform is a centralized platform that utilizes artificial intelligence to create, update, and disseminate team knowledge and documentation. The platform will use natural language processing to analyze and generate insights from existing documentation, reducing the time and effort required to maintain accurate and comprehensive knowledge bases.

## Revenue Model
The revenue model will be based on a monthly SaaS subscription, with tiered pricing based on the number of users and features required.

## MVP Format
Minimum Viable Product:

- User authentication and authorization
- Basic documentation creation and editing capabilities
- AI-powered document analysis and suggestions
- Integration with existing corporate databases and systems

## Estimated Build Time
6-8 months (including development, testing, and deployment)

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct surveys and interviews with IT managers and Operations leads to gauge interest and understand pain points.
2. Develop a prototype and conduct usability testing with a small group of users.
3. Deploy the MVP and gather feedback from pilot customers.
4. Continuously gather and incorporate user feedback to iterate and improve the platform.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React.js, WebSockets for real-time collaboration
- Backend: Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB for data storage
- AI/ML: Google Cloud AI Platform, TensorFlow for natural language processing
- Integration: API connectors for existing corporate systems and databases
