## Problem
Remote company founders and operations managers struggle to find reliable part-time website maintenance specialists for ongoing upkeep and optimization. This leads to a lack of expertise and time to focus on business growth, impacting revenue and customer satisfaction.

## Target User
- Remote company founders
- Operations managers
- Businesses with 1-10 team members

## Solution
TeamWeb is a talent marketplace that connects remote companies with pre-vetted, part-time website maintenance specialists for ongoing upkeep and optimization. This solution enables businesses to find and hire reliable professionals for website maintenance tasks such as theme and plugin updates, security patches, content uploads, SEO optimization, and performance monitoring.

## Revenue Model
TeamWeb operates a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering various tiers depending on the business's needs and size. This includes additional services such as website audit and consultation, training for internal team members, and ongoing support.

## MVP Format
MVP features will include:

- User-friendly web application for remote companies to submit job postings and communicate with pre-vetted specialists
- Profile system for specialists to showcase their skills, experience, and portfolio
- Matching algorithm to pair businesses with suitable specialists based on task requirements
- Secure payment processing and invoicing system

## Estimated Build Time
8-10 weeks (with a dedicated team of developers and designers)

## Validation Steps
1. Research existing talent marketplaces and website maintenance services for remote companies
2. Conduct surveys and interviews with target users to validate the market need
3. Create a working prototype to test the user experience and specialist matching algorithm
4. Conduct user testing and gather feedback for iteration and improvement

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React.js for client-side application development
- Backend: Node.js with Express.js and MongoDB for scalable and secure data storage and management
- Payment Processing: Stripe for smooth and secure transactions
- Matching Algorithm: Combination of machine learning libraries and custom algorithm for efficient pairing suggestions
