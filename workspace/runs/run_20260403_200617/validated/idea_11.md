## Problem
Hiring Managers for growing SaaS companies struggle to find qualified Sales Development Representative (SDR) candidates due to the high volume of applications and the need to filter out unqualified candidates manually.

## Target User
Hiring Managers for growing SaaS companies, particularly those in the sales and recruitment department.

## Solution
Automated Sdr CandidateAI is an AI-powered platform that sources and filters SDR candidates from remote job boards, combines their skills assessment and client feedback databases to provide a comprehensive profile of the candidates' qualifications and performance.

## Revenue Model
The platform operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering various tiers of access to premium features and support.

## MVP Format
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will feature:
1. Data integration with top remote job boards.
2. Basic skills assessment and candidate filtering.
3. Limited client feedback database.

## Estimated Build Time
8 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Define the scope of the platform and its key features.
2. Conduct market research to identify target customers and their pain points.
3. Develop a proof-of-concept prototype to demonstrate the platform's capabilities.
4. Conduct user testing and gather feedback to refine the platform.

## Tech Stack
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB
- **Frontend:** React, Redux,Material-UI
- **AI/ML:** TensorFlow.js, Scikit-learn
- **API Integration:** Zapier, OAuth 2.0
- **Cloud:** Amazon Web Services (AWS)
