## Problem
Notion users and managers often experience fragmented workflows, frequent context switching, and an inability to find the information they need quickly. This leads to decreased productivity, mistakes, and missed deadlines.

## Target User
- Notion users who manage multiple projects and tasks.
- Project managers seeking to streamline their workflows and collaboration.
- Business owners and teams that rely on Notion for content management and workflow automation.

## Solution
Notion TaskAI is a Notion-powered task and collaboration hub that integrates relevant features and tools into a single, seamless experience. It streamlines workflows, minimizes context switching, and enables users to find the information they need instantly. Notion TaskAI includes features such as:

- Task automation and workflow management.
- Integrated project timeline and Gantt charts.
- Real-time team collaboration and communication.
- Automatic task assignments, reminders, and notifications.
- Centralized knowledge base and documentation.

## Revenue Model
Notion TaskAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering various pricing tiers to cater to different user needs. The revenue streams include:

- Monthly subscription fees for individual and team users.
- Premium features and add-ons for advanced users.
- Notion API integration fees for external tool providers.

## MVP Format
The minimum viable product (MVP) includes the following key features:

- Task automation and workflow management.
- Integrated project timeline and Gantt charts.
- Basic team collaboration and communication features.
- Centralized knowledge base and documentation.

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for Notion TaskAI is 6-8 months, assuming a development team of 4-6 members with experience in Notion API integration, workflow automation, and collaboration features.

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct in-depth market research and analyze Notion user needs and feedback.
2. Gather feedback from potential users and stakeholders to refine the solution.
3. Develop a prototype and conduct usability testing and iteration.
4. Pilot the MVP with a small group of not-yet-released customers.
5. Monitor usage, feedback, and financial metrics to inform future development and roadmap.

## Tech Stack
- Notion API for integration and data mapping.
- Next.js or React for building the frontend application.
- Node.js and Express.js for building the backend API and workflow automation.
- PostgreSQL or MongoDB for database management.
- WebSockets or Socket.io for real-time communication and collaboration.
