## Problem
Digital agencies face significant challenges in finding, engaging, and qualifying top-performing Sales Development Representatives (SDRs) to fill rapid client demand. Inefficient manual processes, limited visibility into existing talent networks, and the lack of a dedicated SDR talent pipeline create bottlenecks in the hiring process, ultimately impacting revenue and agency reputation.

## Target User
The target user is the Sourcing Specialist at a digital agency, responsible for finding and procuring top SDR talent to meet client demands. This individual faces:

* Time-consuming manual searches across multiple job boards and platforms
* Difficulty identifying and engaging top SDR candidates
* Inadequate visibility into existing talent networks
* Pressure to meet aggressive hiring deadlines

## Solution
Client-Specific SDR TalentAI is a dedicated SDR talent pipeline platform that streamlines sourcing, engagement, and qualification of top SDR talent from remote job boards and existing talent networks. Key features include:

* Client-specific SDR talent search and qualification engine
* Integration with multiple job boards and existing talent networks
* Sourcing automation and AI-driven candidate matching
* Automated candidate engagement and qualification workflows
* Real-time analytics and performance dashboards

## Revenue Model
Client-Specific SDR TalentAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, with tiered pricing based on the number of clients, job openings, and talent network integrations.

## MVP Format
Minimum Viable Product (MVP) Format:

1. Client sign-up and onboarding process
2. Core talent search and qualification engine
3. Limited job board integrations (e.g., LinkedIn, Indeed)
4. Basic candidate engagement and qualification workflows
5. Fundamental analytics and performance dashboards

## Estimated Build Time
Estimated build time for the MVP is approximately 8 weeks, assuming a team of 3-4 developers with the necessary expertise in automation, AI, and SaaS platform development.

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct user interviews with 5-10 Sourcing Specialists at digital agencies to validate the problem and gather input on the solution.
2. Develop a prototype and conduct usability testing with a small group of users (n=3-5).
3. Collect feedback and iterate on the prototype before launching the MVP.
4. Conduct a soft launch with a small set of pilot clients to gather feedback and validate the solution in real-world scenarios.

## Tech Stack
- Server-side: Node.js, Express.js
- Frontend: React.js, Redux
- Database: PostgreSQL
- AI/ML Engine: TensorFlow.js or similar
- Integration: API connectors for job boards and talent networks (e.g., Zapier, MuleSoft)
