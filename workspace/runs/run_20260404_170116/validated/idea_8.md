## Problem
Remote Recruitment Managers at startups in the SaaS or MarTech space struggle to identify and hire qualified Sales Development Representatives (SDRs). The assessment and evaluation process of SDR candidates is often subjective, lengthy, and prone to human biases, resulting in costly mis-hires and productivity losses.

## Target User
Remote Recruitment Managers at startups in the SaaS or MarTech space responsible for filling Sales Development Representative (SDR) roles.

## Solution
SalesAI is a user-friendly AI-driven platform designed to assess and match SDR candidates to open positions based on their core competencies, cultural fit, and work style. The platform will evaluate SDR candidates through a series of AI-powered assessments, including behavioral interviews, skills evaluations, and cultural fit quizzes.

## Revenue Model
SalesAI will operate on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering tiered pricing plans for varying levels of support and features.

## MVP Format
Minimum Viable Product (MVP) features:

- AI-powered assessment tools for SDR core competencies, cultural fit, and work style
- Candidate matching and shortlisting based on job requirements and needs
- Integration with popular HR systems and applicant tracking systems (ATS)
- Basic analytics and reporting on SDR performance and candidate pipeline

## Estimated Build Time
12 weeks (3 months)

## Validation Steps
1. Pilot the platform with a small group of beta customers to gather feedback and refine features
2. Conduct A/B testing of AI-powered assessment tools to ensure accuracy and fairness
3. Integrate with key HR and ATS providers to streamline workflow and adoption

## Tech Stack
- Backend: Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB
- Frontend: React, HTML, CSS
- AI/ML: TensorFlow.js, IBM Watson
- Integration: API connectors for HR and ATS platforms
