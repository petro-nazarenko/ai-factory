## Problem
Hiring Sales Development Representatives (SDRs) can be a challenging and time-consuming process for managers of growing SaaS, MarTech, FinTech, or EdTech companies. The process involves sifting through countless resumes, conducting interviews, and assessing skills, which can lead to long hiring cycles, high recruitment costs, and a lack of qualified candidates.

## Target User
The target user for PlatformAI is a hiring manager or director of sales at fast-growing SaaS, MarTech, FinTech, or EdTech companies. This person is responsible for leading the sales team and is under pressure to hire top talent quickly and efficiently.

## Solution
PlatformAI is a comprehensive hiring platform that connects hiring managers with highly skilled Sales Development Representative candidates. The platform offers a curated marketplace, streamlining the hiring process by:

* Providing a pool of pre-vetted, top SDR candidates
* Offering AI-powered matchmaking to ensure the best fit for the role
* Reducing the time spent on screening, interviewing, and assessing candidates
* Enabling seamless integration with existing HR systems

## Revenue Model
PlatformAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, offering a tiered pricing structure based on the number of users, candidates, and features required. This model ensures a predictable revenue stream, allowing the platform to continuously improve and expand its services.

## MVP Format
MVP (Minimum Viable Product) for PlatformAI will include:

* A curated marketplace with a pool of pre-vetted SDR candidates
* Basic AI-powered matching engine to suggest top candidates for each role
* Integration with common HR systems for easy candidate management
* Simple user dashboard for easy access to candidate information and matching results

## Estimated Build Time
The estimated build time for the MVP is approximately 6-8 months, assuming a 2-person development team with a mix of automation, SaaS, and DevOps expertise.

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct competitor research to understand the current marketplace and existing solutions.
2. Develop a basic AI-powered matching engine to test its efficacy in finding top SDR candidates.
3. Collaborate with 5-10 target customers to gather feedback on the MVP and iterate on the platform's features and user experience.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React, Redux
- Backend: Node.js, Express
- Database: PostgreSQL
- AI/ML: TensorFlow.js, OpenCV
- Integration: Zapier, REST APIs
