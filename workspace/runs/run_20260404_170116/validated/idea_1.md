## Problem
Recruitment processes for underrepresented engineers in the tech industry take an inordinate amount of time and often result in bias towards a small pool of well-connected individuals, hindering diverse talent acquisition for large companies.

## Target User
Talent Acquisition Managers at large corporations struggle to find underrepresented engineers, leading to talent shortages, costly recruitment processes, and negative impacts on company diversity and inclusion.

## Solution
Recruitment PlatformAI uses AI-powered matchmaking to connect companies with underrepresented engineers in the tech industry, streamlining talent acquisition and reducing bias and wasted recruitment efforts.

## Revenue Model
Revenue generated through monthly SaaS subscription model for companies, requiring companies to create an account, upload required information about job openings, and pay a flat monthly fee for access to the AI-driven matchmaking engine.

## MVP Format
Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include:

* AI-powered job matching engine
* Database of diverse engineers' profiles and skill sets
* Basic company account creation and job posting capabilities
* Matching and pipeline management tools
* Integration with existing job posting platforms (e.g. LinkedIn, Indeed)

## Estimated Build Time
12 weeks

## Validation Steps
1. Conduct market research to determine feasibility and competition for AI-driven recruitment platforms.
2. Develop and test AI matching engine using dataset of diverse engineers' profiles and skill sets.
3. Pilot the MVP with 10 large corporations and 50 diverse engineers.
4. Collect feedback and iterate on MVP.

## Tech Stack
- Backend: Python with Flask API, utilizing Natural Language Processing (NLP) and machine learning algorithms for AI-powered matchmaking.
- Database: MySQL for storing company and diverse engineers' profiles, and job posting information.
- Frontend: JavaScript and React for creating user-friendly interface for job postings, match pipeline management, and account creation.
- Machine Learning Model: Training and deployment of AI-powered matching engine using Scikit-learn and TensorFlow.
