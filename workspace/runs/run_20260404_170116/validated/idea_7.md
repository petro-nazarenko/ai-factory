## Problem
Mid-sized companies struggle with finding and hiring top-performing Sales Development Representatives (SDRs) for remote onboarding, resulting in a high turnover rate, decreased sales performance, and wasted resources.

## Target User
Talent Acquisition Managers at mid-sized companies in the US and UK, responsible for recruiting and hiring SDRs, struggle to find top talent due to:

*   Inadequate time and resources
*   Difficulty accessing a pool of highly skilled SDR candidates
*   High burn rate in recruiting and onboard process

## Solution
TalentAI is a specialized talent sourcing platform that:

*   Utilizes AI-driven matchmaking to pair high-caliber SDRs with the right companies
*   Provides pre-screened, handpicked candidates for seamless remote onboarding
*   Offers insights and analytics to inform talent acquisition decisions

## Revenue Model
TalentAI operates on a monthly SaaS subscription model, where companies pay a flat fee per SDR candidate submitted or hired.

## MVP Format
Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include:

*   Basic talent matching algorithm
*   A curated pool of 100 pre-screened SDR candidates
*   Integration with popular HR systems for seamless onboarding

## Estimated Build Time
Estimated development time for MVP: 3 months (~720 hours)

## Validation Steps
1.  Develop and pilot-test the talent matching algorithm using a small pool of SDR candidate data
2.  Integrate with at least 3 popular HR systems for onboarding
3.  Conduct customer discovery interviews with Talent Acquisition Managers to refine product features and pricing

## Tech Stack
-   Node.js
-   Express.js
-   MongoDB
-   React.js for front-end development
-   AWS for cloud hosting and infrastructure
